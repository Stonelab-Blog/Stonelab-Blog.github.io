## Selecting the Right Stone for Your Space

Choosing the perfect stone for your project requires consideration of aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. Here are StoneLab’s expert tips:

- **For kitchens**: Opt for durable materials such as quartzite or granite with subtle gold highlights to withstand daily use while adding visual interest.
- **For bathrooms**: Select marble varieties with naturally occurring golden veining, complemented by gold-toned fixtures for a cohesive look.
- **For living areas and furniture**: Consider bespoke pieces crafted from limestone or travertine with custom guld inlays for a refined, personalized touch.

When selecting your stone, always consider the interplay of color, texture, and finish to ensure harmony with the overall design vision.

## Stone Care and Maintenance: Keeping Surfaces Radiant

To preserve the beauty of your interior stone design, StoneLab recommends:

- **Regular gentle cleaning**: Use pH-neutral cleaners to prevent etching and maintain the surface’s natural luster.
- **Prompt spill management**: Blot spills immediately, especially on porous stones like marble, to avoid staining.
- **Annual resealing**: For high-traffic surfaces, reseal once a year to protect against moisture and maintain the brilliance of both stone and gold accents.

With proper care, your exclusive stone interiors will remain striking for decades.

### What makes StoneLab’s custom stone solutions unique in Sweden and Scandinavia?

### How do guld accents enhance interior stone design?
Guld (gold) accents provide a luxurious contrast to natural stone surfaces, adding warmth, elegance, and a unique visual appeal to kitchen stone countertops, marble bathroom designs, and bespoke stone furniture.

### Are StoneLab’s materials sustainably sourced?
Yes, StoneLab is committed to sustainable stone sourcing, prioritizing ethical quarrying practices and local or regional materials to reduce carbon footprint while maintaining high-quality luxury interior materials.

### What types of stone are best for kitchen countertops with gold highlights?
