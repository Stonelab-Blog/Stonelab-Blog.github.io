## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Every Space in Scandinavia and Beyond

Choosing the right stone is essential to achieving both aesthetic harmony and practical performance in luxury interiors throughout Scandinavia and Northern Europe:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust, stain-resistant stone such as granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops. These surfaces combine beauty with ease of maintenance.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic choice, but consider honed or matte finishes for a modern twist and added slip resistance.
- **Living Areas:** For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, choose unique veining or colorations that create a focal point without overwhelming the space.

### Practical Selection Tips for Luxury Homeowners and Interior Designers

- **Evaluate Lifestyle Needs:** Consider usage patterns—families may benefit from more resilient stones, while statement pieces can showcase more delicate varieties.
- **Coordinate with Other Luxury Interior Materials:** Pair stone with wood, metal, or glass for a balanced, layered look.
- **Consult an Expert:** StoneLab’s team provides tailored guidance to ensure each selection aligns with your vision and requirements.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the pristine appearance of your stone interiors is simple with the right approach:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to protect the stone’s surface.
- **Seal Regularly:** Many stones, such as marble and limestone, benefit from periodic sealing to guard against stains and moisture.
- **Prompt Attention:** Wipe spills immediately, especially acidic substances, to prevent etching or discoloration.
