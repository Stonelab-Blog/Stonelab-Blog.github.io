## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone for your project involves both aesthetic and practical considerations. Here are some expert recommendations from StoneLab:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for dense, non-porous materials like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops, combining beauty with resilience.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a classic choice for its elegance, but consider sealed quartzite for high-traffic areas to ensure lasting perfection.
- **Living Spaces:** For bespoke stone furniture and accent walls, explore unique patterns and colors that complement your overall design scheme.

## Care and Maintenance Tips

To preserve the pristine look of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Seal Surfaces:** Regularly seal natural stone to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Gentle Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid scratching or dulling the finish.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe up spills promptly, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus.
