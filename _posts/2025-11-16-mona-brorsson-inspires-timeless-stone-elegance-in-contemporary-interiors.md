### Choosing the Perfect Stone for Each Space in Sweden and Beyond

Selecting the right stone begins with understanding the unique demands of each room:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for durable materials like granite or quartzite for countertops that resist staining and wear, ensuring longevity in high-traffic spaces.
- **Bathroom:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic, but consider sealed limestone or engineered stone for areas exposed to moisture.
- **Living Areas:** For bespoke stone furniture and feature walls, choose stones with unique veining or color variations to create a focal point.
- **Outdoor Spaces:** Weather-resistant stones such as slate or sandstone offer both beauty and resilience for terraces and patios.

### Keeping Stone Surfaces Pristine

Luxury interior materials demand attentive care to preserve their elegance. Here are a few essential tips:

- **Clean gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners to avoid damaging the stone’s surface.
- **Seal regularly:** Most natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Prevent scratches:** Place felt pads under furniture and avoid abrasive cleaning tools.
- **Wipe spills promptly:** Especially important for porous stones like marble.
