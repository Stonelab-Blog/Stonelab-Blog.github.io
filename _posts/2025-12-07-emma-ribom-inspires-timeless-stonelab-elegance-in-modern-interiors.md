## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the ideal stone is essential for achieving a harmonious and functional interior. StoneLab recommends the following considerations:

### **Kitchen Stone Countertops**
- **Best Choices:** Quartzite, granite, and marble.
- **Tip:** Opt for stones with high resistance to staining and scratching for a practical yet luxurious workspace.

### **Marble Bathroom Design**
- **Best Choices:** Light-colored marbles for a spa-like ambiance.
- **Tip:** Choose honed or matte finishes to reduce slipperiness and enhance comfort.

### **Bespoke Stone Furniture**
- **Best Choices:** Carefully matched slabs and contrasting textures.
- **Tip:** Let the natural veining of the stone become an artistic focal point, particularly in living rooms or entryways.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

To preserve the elegance of your interior stone design, StoneLab recommends:
- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid surface damage.
- **Sealing:** Periodic sealing protects against stains, especially in high-traffic areas.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Address spills quickly to prevent etching or discoloration.
- **Professional Restoration:** Schedule occasional professional maintenance to restore polish and repair minor imperfections.
