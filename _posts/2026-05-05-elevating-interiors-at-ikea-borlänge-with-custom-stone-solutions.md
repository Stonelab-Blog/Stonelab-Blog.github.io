## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Luxury Home or Project

Selecting the perfect stone depends on both aesthetics and functionality. Consider these guidelines tailored for luxury homeowners, interior designers, architects, developers, high-end furniture designers, and real estate staging professionals across Scandinavia, Germany, and the UK:

### Kitchens

- **Recommended Stones:** Quartzite, granite, and engineered stone offer exceptional resistance to scratches and stains.
- **Design Tip:** Integrate kitchen stone countertops with waterfall edges for a seamless, modern effect.

### Bathrooms

- **Recommended Stones:** Marble and limestone create a spa-like ambiance, ideal for luxury bathroom interiors.
- **Design Tip:** Choose large-format slabs for vanities and wall cladding to minimize grout lines and emphasize natural patterns.

### Living Spaces and Furniture

- **Recommended Stones:** Travertine and onyx make striking bespoke stone furniture, from side tables to statement fireplace surrounds.
- **Design Tip:** Consider stone with unique veining or coloration to serve as a focal point in your living area.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty of Your Exclusive Stone Interiors

To preserve the elegance of your exclusive stone interiors crafted by StoneLab:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Wipe surfaces with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner.
- **Seal Regularly:** Especially important for marble bathroom design and other porous stones to prevent staining.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals and abrasive pads that can dull the finish or scratch the surface.
- **Address Spills Promptly:** Remove acidic substances like wine or citrus quickly to prevent etching.
