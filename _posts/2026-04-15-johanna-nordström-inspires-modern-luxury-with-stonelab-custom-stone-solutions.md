## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone begins with understanding the needs and character of each room.

### Kitchens

- **Popular choices:** Quartzite, granite, and durable marbles.
- **Advice:** Opt for stones with high resistance to heat and staining for kitchen stone countertops. Matte finishes offer a modern edge and are forgiving of daily use.

### Bathrooms

- **Popular choices:** Polished marble and limestone for a spa-like experience.
- **Advice:** Choose stones with low porosity for marble bathroom designs to minimize water absorption. Consider slip-resistant finishes in wet areas.

### Living Areas & Furniture

- **Popular choices:** Travertine and bespoke stone furniture in unique shapes and textures.
- **Advice:** Custom stone solutions can highlight focal points—think feature fireplace mantels or dramatic coffee tables.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty

StoneLab’s exclusive stone interiors are crafted to stand the test of time, but proper care enhances longevity:

- **Clean regularly:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to protect the stone’s finish.
- **Seal as needed:** Most natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to guard against stains and moisture.
- **Avoid abrasives:** Never use harsh scrubbing pads or acidic cleaners, which can damage the surface.
- **Wipe spills promptly:** Especially important for marble and limestone, which can be sensitive to acidic substances.
