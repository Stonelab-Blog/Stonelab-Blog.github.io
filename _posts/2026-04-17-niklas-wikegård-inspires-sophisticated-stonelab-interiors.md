## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are expert tips from StoneLab to guide your decisions:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops. These materials are highly resistant to stains and scratches, ensuring beauty and practicality.
- **Bathroom:** Marble remains a classic choice for bathrooms, offering a luxurious look. Ensure it is professionally sealed to protect against moisture.
- **Living Areas:** Consider bespoke stone furniture designed to suit your space. Travertine and limestone add a softer, understated elegance.
- **Accent Walls and Fireplaces:** Use dramatic, high-impact stones such as onyx or richly veined marble to create stunning features.

## Care and Maintenance for Timeless Beauty

To preserve the allure of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Clean regularly** with pH-neutral cleaners and avoid abrasive pads.
- **Seal surfaces** as recommended, especially in wet areas.
- **Wipe spills promptly** to prevent staining, particularly with marble and limestone.
- **Use trivets and mats** on kitchen stone countertops to protect from heat and scratches.
