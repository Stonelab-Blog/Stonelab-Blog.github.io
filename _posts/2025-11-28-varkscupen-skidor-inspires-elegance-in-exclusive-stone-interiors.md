## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Luxury Interior Space

Choosing the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Consider these expert tips for different areas of the home:

### Kitchen Stone Countertops and Surfaces

- **Countertops:** Select dense, low-porosity stones such as granite or quartzite for durability and stain resistance. A matte or polished finish enhances both performance and style.
- **Backsplashes:** Marble or limestone in subtle veining adds elegance and is easy to maintain with regular sealing.

### Marble Bathroom Design and Stone Features

- **Vanities and Walls:** Marble bathroom design remains a favorite for its luxurious feel. Opt for water-resistant stones and ensure expert installation for lasting beauty.
- **Floors:** Non-slip finishes and lighter tones enhance both safety and the sense of space.

### Bespoke Stone Furniture and Living Spaces

- **Fireplaces and Feature Walls:** Dramatic stonework can become the centerpiece of a room. Custom-cut panels and intricate inlays showcase true Swedish stone craftsmanship.
- **Furniture:** Bespoke stone furniture pieces, such as coffee tables or benches, fuse durability with artistry.

## Care and Maintenance for Luxury Interior Materials

Luxury interior materials like stone require thoughtful care to maintain their original luster:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damage.
- **Sealing:** Apply a high-quality sealant periodically, especially in kitchens and bathrooms, to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Immediate Attention:** Address spills promptly—especially acidic substances—to prevent etching or discoloration.
- **Professional Care:** Schedule regular inspections and, if needed, professional polishing to preserve the stone's pristine appearance.
