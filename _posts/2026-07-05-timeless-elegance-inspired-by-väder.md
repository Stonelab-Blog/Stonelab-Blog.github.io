## How to Select the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone for your home or project is a journey in aesthetics and function:

- **Kitchens:** Quartzite and granite kitchen stone countertops offer durability and timeless appeal, balancing style with practicality.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a coveted choice, with StoneLab’s expertise ensuring both luxury and longevity.
- **Living areas:** Bespoke stone furniture pieces—think sculpted coffee tables or shelving—bring sophistication and natural warmth.
- **Entryways and feature walls:** Large-format stone panels create an immediate impression, setting the tone for the rest of your home.

### Practical Advice for Stone Selection

1. **Consider the space’s use:** High-traffic zones benefit from harder stones, while decorative areas allow for more delicate materials.
2. **Think about maintenance:** Polished stones elevate formality, while honed finishes offer a softer, more lived-in feel.
3. **Embrace individuality:** Every slab is unique—work with StoneLab’s experts to select pieces that reflect your personal style.

## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Stone Surfaces Beautiful

Luxury interior materials deserve attentive care. To preserve the elegance of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Seal regularly:** Protect surfaces from moisture and stains by resealing as recommended.
- **Clean with gentle products:** Avoid harsh chemicals; opt for pH-neutral cleaners specifically formulated for stone.
- **Address spills promptly:** Especially on marble and lighter stones, quick action ensures surfaces remain pristine.
- **Use coasters and mats:** Prevent scratches and etching on kitchen stone countertops and bespoke stone furniture.
