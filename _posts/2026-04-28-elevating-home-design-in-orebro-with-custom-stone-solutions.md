### Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space in Luxury Scandinavian Interiors

Selecting the perfect stone is critical to achieving both aesthetic harmony and practical longevity. Consider the following guidance for different areas of the home:

- **Kitchens:** For kitchen stone countertops, durability and resistance to staining are key. Granite and quartzite offer robust performance, while marble delivers unparalleled elegance for less demanding prep zones.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a hallmark of luxury. Opt for sealed, water-resistant finishes to maintain a pristine look in high-moisture environments.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture and accent walls in travertine, limestone, or granite lend gravitas without overwhelming the room’s ambiance.
- **Floors and Stairs:** Hardwearing stones like slate and granite ensure longevity and safety for high-traffic areas.

### Care and Maintenance Tips for Exclusive Stone Interiors

StoneLab’s custom stone solutions are crafted to endure, but a few simple practices will keep surfaces looking their best:

- **Clean Gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid dulling the finish or damaging the stone.
- **Seal Regularly:** Especially for marble and limestone, periodic sealing prevents stains and moisture absorption.
- **Protect Against Scratches:** Place felt pads under heavy objects and avoid dragging items across stone surfaces.
