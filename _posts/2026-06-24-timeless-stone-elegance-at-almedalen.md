## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Luxury Home

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Consider these expert tips from StoneLab:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones such as granite or quartzite for countertops, ensuring resistance to stains and scratches. Matte finishes are trending for their understated elegance.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic, offering a sense of spa-like luxury. Choose sealed, honed marble for wet areas to maintain beauty and longevity.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture—such as coffee tables, benches, or mantelpieces—adds a sculptural element and can be customized to suit any scale or style.
- **Entryways:** Durable stones like limestone or slate create a welcoming, high-impact entrance that stands up to daily use.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty of Stone Interiors

To preserve the exquisite finish of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Clean Regularly:** Use a pH-neutral cleaner and soft cloth; avoid abrasive pads or acidic solutions.
- **Seal Periodically:** Most natural stones benefit from professional sealing to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Prevent Damage:** Use coasters and mats on kitchen stone countertops, and promptly wipe spills—especially acidic substances like citrus or wine.
- **Routine Inspection:** Look for signs of wear or etching, and consult experts for restoration if needed.
