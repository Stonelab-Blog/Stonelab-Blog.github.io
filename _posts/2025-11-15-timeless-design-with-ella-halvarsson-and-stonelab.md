## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone for your interior is both an art and a science. Consider the following expert advice for selecting the best material for each area:

### Kitchen

- **Granite and Quartzite**: Highly durable and resistant to stains, perfect for kitchen stone countertops that see daily use.
- **Marble**: Offers unmatched elegance, but requires regular sealing and care to maintain its pristine look.

### Bathroom

- **Marble and Limestone**: These stones bring a luxurious, calming atmosphere. Opt for honed finishes to minimize water marks and slips.
- **Porcelain panels with stone-like finishes**: For a modern twist and easier maintenance.

### Living and Dining Spaces

- **Custom stone furniture**: Coffee tables, sideboards, and shelving in dramatic stone varieties add luxury and personality.
- **Feature walls**: Large-format stone panels create stunning visual impact and elevate the entire ambiance.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the pristine look of your exclusive stone interiors requires consistent care:

- **Clean regularly** with a soft cloth and pH-neutral stone cleaner.
- **Seal natural stone** surfaces annually to protect against stains and etching.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals** and abrasive pads that can damage the finish.
- **Blot spills immediately** to prevent long-term discoloration, especially with marble.

StoneLab provides tailored care instructions for every installation, ensuring your investment remains as stunning as the day it was crafted.
