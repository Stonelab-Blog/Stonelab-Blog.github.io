## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone requires a balance of aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. Here’s how to approach each space:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for countertops—these resist staining and withstand heavy use while offering striking visual appeal.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble and limestone evoke spa-like luxury, but require proper sealing to maintain their finish in humid conditions.
- **Living Spaces:** For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, consider unique stones such as travertine or Swedish granite for their character and heritage.
- **Outdoors:** Weather-resistant stones like slate or sandstone are ideal for terraces and garden features.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To ensure the longevity of your exclusive stone interiors, regular care is essential:

- **Clean with mild, pH-neutral cleaners**—avoid acids or harsh chemicals.
- **Seal porous stones** annually to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Wipe spills promptly** and use coasters or mats under glasses and hot dishes.
- **Dust regularly** to maintain the natural sheen of your stone surfaces.

### Which types of stone are best suited for kitchen countertops and bathrooms?  
For kitchen countertops, durable stones like granite and engineered quartz are ideal due to their resistance to staining and wear. Marble and limestone are preferred in bathrooms for their luxurious appearance but require proper sealing to withstand humid environments.
