## Exploring Current Trends in Stone Interiors

Leading designers, including Anders Blomquist, are championing a new wave of luxury interior materials characterized by natural textures, subtle veining, and an emphasis on sustainability. Key trends shaping the niche include:

## How to Select the Right Stone for Your Space

Choosing the ideal stone is essential for both aesthetics and longevity. Consider these expert tips:

- **Purpose and Durability:** High-traffic areas like kitchens benefit from granite or quartzite, while marble is perfect for bathroom sanctuaries or accent walls.
- **Color and Pattern:** Select hues and veining that complement your interior palette and reflect natural light.
- **Finish:** Polished finishes impart a luxurious sheen, while honed or matte surfaces create a contemporary, understated effect.
- **Sustainability:** Opt for suppliers like StoneLab, who commit to sustainable stone sourcing and responsible production.

## Keeping Stone Surfaces Beautiful

StoneLab’s exclusive stone interiors are designed for enduring beauty. To keep surfaces pristine:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use mild, pH-neutral cleaners and a soft cloth—avoid acidic substances on marble or limestone.
- **Sealing:** Periodically seal stone countertops and bathroom surfaces to prevent staining and etching.
- **Immediate Care:** Wipe spills promptly, especially on porous stones, to maintain flawless surfaces.
- **Professional Maintenance:** For bespoke stone furniture or large installations, schedule periodic check-ups with your stone specialist.
