## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Luxury Interior Design

Selecting the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Consider these expert tips:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust stones like granite or quartzite for countertops, combining luxury interior materials with superior resistance to heat and stains.
- **Bathrooms:** Choose marble for its timeless elegance, or explore limestone for a softer, spa-like ambiance.
- **Living areas & furniture:** Bespoke stone furniture and feature walls create conversation pieces without overpowering the room.
- **Entryways:** Polish and finish options can be tailored for durability and slip resistance, perfect for high-traffic zones.

## Caring for Stone Surfaces in Exclusive Stone Interiors

To preserve the pristine look of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use mild, pH-neutral cleaners and avoid harsh chemicals.
- **Sealant application:** Periodic sealing protects porous surfaces from stains and moisture.
- **Gentle maintenance:** Soft cloths and non-abrasive sponges help maintain the natural sheen.
