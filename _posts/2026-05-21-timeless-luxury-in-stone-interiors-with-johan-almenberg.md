## How to Select the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone requires balancing aesthetics, function, and longevity. Here are Johan Almenberg’s recommendations:

### Kitchen
- **Material:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops.
- **Finish:** Matte or honed finishes reduce glare and fingerprints, ideal for high-traffic areas.
- **Tip:** Consider the overall palette—subtle veining in marble can add sophistication without overpowering the room.

### Bathroom
- **Material:** Marble bathroom design continues to be a symbol of luxury, but choose denser stones for wet zones.
- **Finish:** Polished marble creates a spa-like atmosphere, while textured surfaces offer better slip resistance.

### Living Spaces and Furniture
- **Applications:** Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or mantelpieces, can anchor a room with sculptural elegance.
- **Color:** Lighter shades enhance the sense of space, while dramatic dark stones create an intimate, moody ambience.

## Caring for Stone Surfaces

StoneLab’s custom stone solutions are crafted for a lifetime, but a few simple steps ensure your investment remains pristine:

- **Clean regularly:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid etching or scratching.
- **Seal periodically:** Especially for porous stones like marble, sealing protects against stains and moisture.
- **Protect from heat and impact:** Use trivets for hot pans and avoid dropping heavy objects to maintain flawless surfaces.
