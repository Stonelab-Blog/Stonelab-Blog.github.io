## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

The perfect stone solution is as much about aesthetics as it is about function. Here’s how to approach selection for every room:

### Kitchen

- **Ideal Stones:** Durable stones like granite or engineered quartz excel as kitchen stone countertops.
- **Tips:** Opt for lighter tones to enhance natural light, or dramatic dark slabs for contrast in Scandinavian-inspired kitchens.

### Bathroom

- **Ideal Stones:** Marble lends classic elegance to bathroom design, especially for vanities and feature walls.
- **Tips:** Sealed, honed finishes resist water stains and maintain a luxurious matte look.

### Living Spaces

- **Ideal Stones:** Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or shelving, acts as both art and utility.
- **Tips:** Pair stone with warm woods and soft textiles for inviting, layered interiors.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

To preserve the pristine condition of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damaging natural stone.
- **Sealing:** Reapply sealant periodically, especially for marble and limestone surfaces.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe spills promptly to prevent stains, particularly on lighter stones.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals or scouring pads that can scratch or dull surfaces.
