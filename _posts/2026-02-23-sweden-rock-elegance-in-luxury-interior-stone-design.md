## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the ideal stone involves balancing beauty, functionality, and maintenance needs. Here are tips for key spaces:

### Kitchen

- **Best Stones:** Granite and quartzite for kitchen stone countertops due to their durability and resistance to heat and stains.
- **Style Tip:** Opt for lighter tones to enhance spaciousness or dramatic veining for a bold statement.

### Bathroom

- **Best Stones:** Marble for bathroom design, prized for its elegance; consider honed finishes for a softer, modern look.
- **Style Tip:** Use oversized slabs for a seamless, spa-like effect.

### Living Spaces

- **Best Stones:** Limestone and travertine for bespoke stone furniture and feature walls, offering warmth and subtle sophistication.
- **Style Tip:** Mix textures and finishes to create visual interest and a sense of luxury.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the natural beauty of your stone interiors:

- **Clean regularly** with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaners designed for natural stone.
- **Seal surfaces annually** to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Avoid abrasive sponges** and harsh chemicals, which can damage the stone’s finish.
- **Wipe spills promptly,** especially acidic substances like wine or citrus.
