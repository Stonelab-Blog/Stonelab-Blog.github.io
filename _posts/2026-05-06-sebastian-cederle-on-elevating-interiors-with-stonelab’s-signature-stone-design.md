## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Scandinavian Luxury Home

Choosing the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are Sebastian Cederle’s expert tips:

- **For kitchens**: Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite for countertops. Marble adds elegance but benefits from regular maintenance.
- **For bathrooms**: Marble and onyx create a spa-like ambiance, while textured finishes prevent slipping.
- **For living areas**: Bespoke stone furniture introduces luxury and individuality, with options ranging from coffee tables to wall cladding.
- **For eco-conscious projects**: Consider stones sourced through StoneLab’s sustainable supply chain, ensuring beauty without compromise.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty in Exclusive Stone Interiors

Even the most exclusive stone interiors require thoughtful care. To preserve their pristine appearance:

- **Clean gently**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid etching or scratching.
- **Seal surfaces**: Regular sealing protects against stains and moisture, especially in high-use areas like kitchen countertops.
- **Preventative habits**: Use coasters, avoid acidic spills, and address messes promptly to maintain natural stone’s brilliance.
