## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Your Space

Choosing the right stone involves more than aesthetics. Consider these practical factors to ensure your design stands the test of time:

- **Functionality:** For high-traffic areas like kitchen countertops, opt for durable materials such as granite or engineered stone.
- **Aesthetics:** Marble remains a classic for bathroom design, lending a sense of luxury and purity.
- **Maintenance Needs:** Some stones require more care—discuss options with your StoneLab advisor to find the ideal balance.
- **Sustainability:** Ask about StoneLab’s sustainable stone sourcing, ensuring your investment supports ethical and environmentally friendly practices.

## Caring for Stone Surfaces

With the right care, stonework retains its beauty for generations. Here are essential maintenance tips:

- **Seal Regularly:** Protect porous stones by applying a high-quality sealant as recommended.
- **Clean Gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damaging the surface.
- **Prevent Stains:** Wipe spills immediately, especially for marble and lighter stones.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals or rough sponges.

StoneLab provides clients with tailored care instructions to match each exclusive stone interior, ensuring lasting brilliance.
