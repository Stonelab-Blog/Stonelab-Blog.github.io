The Scandinavian design ethos—marked by clean lines, organic textures, and understated elegance—continues to influence interior stone design in Sweden and across Northern Europe:

## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Northern Europe and the UK

Selecting the ideal stone for your project is an art that balances aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. Here are StoneLab’s expert tips for clients in Sweden, Scandinavia, Germany, and the UK:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for granite or quartzite kitchen stone countertops for superior durability and stain resistance, ideal for busy family homes or professional-grade spaces.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic choice, offering a spa-like ambiance. For high-traffic bathrooms, consider non-porous options to reduce maintenance.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture and accent walls crafted from rare or locally sourced materials add sophistication and individuality.

## Stone Care and Maintenance Tips for Luxury Stone Interiors

Maintain the natural allure of your stone interiors with these simple guidelines:

- Use pH-neutral cleaners designed for stone surfaces.
- Wipe spills promptly, especially on porous stones like marble.
- Seal countertops and bathroom surfaces regularly to preserve their finish.
- Avoid abrasive tools; opt for soft cloths and gentle brushes.

With proper care, your exclusive stone interiors will retain their elegance for years to come.
