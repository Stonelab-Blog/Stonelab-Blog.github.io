  Large-format slabs and sculptural pieces are trending, emphasizing the natural veining and coloration unique to each stone. This approach brings a gallery-like sophistication to living spaces.

- **Sustainable Stone Sourcing**  
  Increasingly, both professionals and homeowners seek eco-conscious materials. StoneLab is at the forefront, offering sustainable stone sourcing to ensure luxury doesn’t come at the expense of the environment.

- **Mixed Material Pairings**  
  Combining stone with wood, metal, or glass creates striking contrasts and tactile interest, making interiors feel both modern and inviting.

- **Personalized Finishes**  
  Matte, honed, or textured finishes allow for further customization, ensuring each project feels truly bespoke.

### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone involves balancing aesthetics, function, and practicality. Here’s what to consider:

- **Kitchens**  
  Opt for durable stones such as granite or quartzite for countertops. These materials withstand daily use while showcasing luxury interior materials.

- **Bathrooms**  
  Marble bathroom design remains a classic, but consider limestone or terrazzo for a softer, contemporary feel. Ensure proper sealing to protect against moisture.

- **Living Areas & Furniture**  
  Bespoke stone furniture, from coffee tables to shelving, adds unique character. Lighter stones can help brighten Scandinavian interiors, while darker hues bring drama and depth.

### Care and Maintenance Tips

To keep your stone surfaces looking impeccable:

- Wipe up spills promptly, especially on porous stones like marble.
- Use pH-neutral cleaners; avoid harsh chemicals.
- Reseal surfaces as recommended, typically every 1–2 years.
- Place mats or trivets under hot cookware on kitchen stone countertops.
