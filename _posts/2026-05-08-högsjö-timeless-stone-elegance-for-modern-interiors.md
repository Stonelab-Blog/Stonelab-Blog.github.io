### Choosing the Right Stone for Your Luxury Interior Project

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are some expert tips to guide your decision:

- **Consider usage and durability:** For high-traffic areas like kitchens, opt for hardwearing materials such as granite or engineered stone for countertops.
- **Embrace individuality:** Each slab of natural stone is unique—choose a pattern and color that resonates with your personal style.
- **Think about ambiance:** Lighter stones like Swedish marble can open up a space, while darker granite adds drama and depth.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces: Maintenance Tips for Scandinavian Stone Craftsmanship

With the right care, custom stone solutions from StoneLab will retain their beauty for generations:

- **Routine cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleanser to maintain the surface.
- **Seal regularly:** Protect porous stones, such as marble, by resealing every 6–12 months.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic products that can damage the stone’s finish.
