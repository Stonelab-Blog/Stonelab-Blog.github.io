## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone is an art that considers both aesthetics and function. Here’s how to approach different spaces:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite for countertops, combining resilience with visual impact.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a classic choice for its elegance, but consider honed finishes for slip resistance and a contemporary feel.
- **Living Spaces:** Statement walls or bespoke stone furniture can serve as focal points, harnessing the beauty of rare or textured stones.
- **Fireplaces:** Choose heat-resistant stones such as soapstone or limestone, blending safety with sophistication.

At StoneLab, our consultants guide every client through the selection process, ensuring each stone harmonizes with the overall design while meeting specific functional requirements.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Stone’s longevity is enhanced with attentive care. To preserve the pristine finish of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to avoid damaging delicate surfaces.
- **Sealing:** Regularly seal porous stones like marble and limestone to protect against stains.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Acidic substances can etch or dull stone; always use products recommended for natural stone.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Wipe spills immediately, especially on kitchen stone countertops, to prevent staining.
