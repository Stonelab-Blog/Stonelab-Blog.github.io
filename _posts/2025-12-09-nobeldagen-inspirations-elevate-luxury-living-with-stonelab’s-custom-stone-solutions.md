## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space in Luxury Interiors

Choosing the ideal stone requires balancing form, function, and longevity. Consider the following:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable, low-porosity stones like granite or quartzite for countertops—easy to maintain and resilient to daily use.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble remains popular for its luxurious appearance, but StoneLab also offers innovative, moisture-resistant stones that withstand humidity.
- **Living Areas**: Statement pieces—fireplaces, walls, or bespoke furniture—benefit from richly veined marbles, warm limestone, or tactile terrazzo.
- **Outdoor Spaces**: Choose weather-resistant stones that complement Scandinavian landscapes and withstand seasonal changes.

Our team at StoneLab guides clients throughout Sweden, Scandinavia, and Northern Europe, ensuring a tailored match between material and design vision.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty of Stone Interiors

To preserve the elegance of your stone interiors:

- Wipe surfaces regularly with a soft, damp cloth.
- Use pH-neutral cleaners specifically designed for stone.
- Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive sponges.
- Seal porous stones periodically to prevent staining.
- Clean up spills promptly—especially those from acidic substances like wine or lemon juice.

With these simple steps, your stonework’s beauty and integrity endure for years to come.
