### Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the ideal stone involves balancing form, function, and atmosphere. Here are a few expert considerations:

- **Kitchens:** For kitchen stone countertops, prioritize durable stones like granite or quartzite, which resist staining and scratching.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble or limestone creates a spa-like ambiance but requires regular sealing to maintain its pristine appearance.
- **Living Spaces:** Consider bespoke stone furniture to introduce luxury without overwhelming the room—think statement coffee tables or minimalist shelving.
- **Hallways and Entrances:** Hard-wearing stones such as slate or travertine offer practicality paired with visual impact.

### Care and Maintenance Tips for Stone Surfaces

To preserve the elegance of your exclusive stone interiors, regular maintenance is essential:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner; avoid harsh chemicals that can erode natural stone.
- **Sealing:** Periodically reseal surfaces, especially in kitchens and bathrooms, to protect against moisture and stains.
- **Immediate Spill Response:** Wipe spills promptly, particularly on porous stones like marble.
- **Protective Measures:** Use coasters, mats, and soft pads under furniture to prevent scratches and etching.
