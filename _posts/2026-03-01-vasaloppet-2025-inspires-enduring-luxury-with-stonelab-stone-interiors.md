## Practical Advice for Selecting Stone

Choosing the right stone for your project requires expertise and an understanding of both aesthetics and function. Consider these factors:

- **Purpose:** Marble is ideal for bathroom vanities and feature walls, while granite stands up to the demands of kitchen surfaces.
- **Tone and Texture:** Select lighter stones for a sense of airiness, or deeper, dramatic hues for an intimate ambiance.
- **Maintenance:** Some stones, like quartzite, offer superior stain resistance, ideal for busy households.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty and longevity of your stone interiors:

- **Clean regularly** with mild, pH-neutral cleaners.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals** that can erode natural finishes.
- **Seal surfaces** as recommended to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Wipe spills promptly,** especially on marble and limestone, to prevent etching.

By following these simple steps, your StoneLab installation will maintain its elegance for years to come.
