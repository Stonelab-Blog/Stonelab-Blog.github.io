## Choosing the Perfect Stone for Every Space

Selecting the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Consider these expert tips from StoneLab:

### For Kitchens
- **Granite** offers unmatched durability and resists stains—ideal for family kitchens.
- **Quartzite** combines natural beauty with low maintenance, perfect for busy homes.

### For Bathrooms
- **Marble** creates a sense of spa-like luxury; opt for sealed surfaces to prevent moisture absorption.
- **Terrazzo** delivers a playful, contemporary look with excellent resilience.

### For Living Spaces and Furniture
- **Limestone** or **Travertine** provide warmth and subtle elegance for living rooms and bespoke stone furniture.
- **Slate** is a stylish, slip-resistant choice for floors or accent walls.

## Stone Care and Maintenance Essentials

To preserve the beauty of luxury interior materials, StoneLab recommends:
- **Regular Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to avoid surface damage.
- **Sealing:** Apply a high-quality sealant annually for kitchen stone countertops and marble bathroom surfaces.
- **Quick Action:** Wipe spills immediately to prevent staining, especially with acidic foods or beverages.
- **Professional Help:** Consult experts for deep cleaning and restoration of exclusive stone interiors.
