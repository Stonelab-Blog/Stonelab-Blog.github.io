## Current Style Trends in Stone Interiors Across Scandinavia and Germany

The latest trends reflect a move toward natural textures, subtle hues, and statement pieces:

- **Soft, Earthy Palettes:** Muted tones and gentle veining are favored for a calming, spa-like ambiance.
- **Large Format Slabs:** Seamless, expansive surfaces are in high demand for kitchen stone countertops and feature walls.
- **Bespoke Stone Furniture:** Custom tables, benches, and shelving in stone add sculptural sophistication to living spaces.
- **Sustainable Choices:** Clients are increasingly prioritizing responsibly sourced stone, echoing a broader commitment to environmental stewardship.

## How to Choose the Right Stone for Your Space in Luxury Interiors

Selecting the ideal stone involves balancing aesthetics, functionality, and maintenance needs. Here are some expert tips from StoneLab’s interior stone design specialists:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable, low-porosity stones such as granite or engineered quartz for countertops that withstand daily use.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design offers classic luxury, but be sure to seal surfaces to protect against moisture and staining.
- **Living Areas:** For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, consider unique stones like limestone or travertine for subtle texture and warmth.
- **Entryways and Stairs:** Hard-wearing stones such as basalt or slate provide both elegance and resilience in high-traffic areas.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty in Exclusive Stone Interiors

Stone surfaces require thoughtful care to maintain their pristine appearance:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a pH-neutral cleanser and a soft cloth; avoid acidic or abrasive products.
- **Sealing:** Periodically seal natural stone to prevent staining and enhance longevity.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe up spills promptly, especially on marble and limestone, to avoid etching or discoloration.
- **Professional Restoration:** Consult experts for deep cleaning, polishing, or repairs to keep surfaces looking their best.
