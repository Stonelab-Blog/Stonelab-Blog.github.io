## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone involves balancing aesthetics, function, and sustainability. Here are practical tips from our experts:

- **Kitchens**: For kitchen stone countertops, consider durable stones like granite or engineered quartzite. These resist staining and scratching, and their refined finishes suit both modern and classic interiors.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble bathroom design remains a hallmark of luxury, but consider honed or textured finishes for added slip resistance and a contemporary feel.
- **Living Spaces**: For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, explore limestone or travertine for a softer, organic look. Each piece can be customized to complement your overall design vision.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To ensure your exclusive stone interiors remain immaculate:

- **Clean Regularly**: Use a pH-neutral cleaner and a soft cloth for daily maintenance.
- **Seal Surfaces**: High-traffic areas such as kitchen countertops benefit from periodic sealing to prevent staining.
- **Avoid Abrasives**: Refrain from using harsh chemicals or abrasive pads that can dull or scratch the stone.

By following these care tips, your stonework will retain its luxurious finish and stand the test of time.
