## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the ideal stone is crucial for both beauty and performance. Consider the following when specifying materials:

- **Kitchen**: Opt for robust, low-porosity stones like granite or engineered quartz for countertops. Their resilience ensures longevity in high-traffic areas.
- **Bathroom**: Marble bathroom design remains a classic, but consider honed finishes for reduced slip and maintenance.
- **Living Spaces**: For bespoke stone furniture and feature walls, select stones with distinctive veining or unique coloration for a personalized touch.
- **Outdoor Areas**: Weather-resistant stones such as slate and granite are perfect for terraces and patios, blending seamlessly with natural surroundings.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Luxurious Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the beauty of your stone interiors is simple with the right approach:

- **Clean Gently**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and a soft cloth to avoid damaging the surface.
- **Seal Regularly**: Natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to prevent staining and preserve their lustre.
- **Protect from Heat and Scratches**: Always use trivets and cutting boards to safeguard kitchen stone countertops.
- **Address Spills Promptly**: Blot spills immediately, especially on porous stones like marble, to prevent etching or discoloration.
