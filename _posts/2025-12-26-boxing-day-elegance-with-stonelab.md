## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the ideal stone begins with understanding both the functional and aesthetic needs of each room:

### Kitchen

- **Best Choices**: Durable granites, quartzites, and non-porous engineered stones.
- **Why**: These materials resist heat, stains, and scratches—ideal for kitchen stone countertops that see daily use.

### Bathroom

- **Best Choices**: Marble and limestone for elegance, or porcelain for low-maintenance luxury.
- **Why**: Marble bathroom designs exude opulence, while modern finishes ensure easy upkeep and slip-resistance.

### Living and Entertaining Spaces

- **Best Choices**: Bespoke stone furniture in travertine or onyx, statement walls in locally-sourced granite.
- **Why**: These choices create visual interest and showcase your personal style, whether in a grand living room or a quiet reading nook.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty

To preserve the pristine look of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Clean regularly** with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner.
- **Seal surfaces** as recommended to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals** and abrasives that can dull the finish.
- **Promptly wipe spills**, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching.

With proper care, custom stone solutions from StoneLab will maintain their allure for generations.
