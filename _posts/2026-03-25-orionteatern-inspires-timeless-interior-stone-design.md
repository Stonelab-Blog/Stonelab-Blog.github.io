## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone for your project is both an art and a science. Here are a few practical tips from StoneLab’s experts for clients in Sweden and beyond:

### For Kitchens

- **Quartzite & Granite**: Highly durable and resistant to heat, perfect for kitchen stone countertops that endure daily use.
- **Marble**: Ideal for statement islands or accent walls, especially when sealed and cared for with diligence.

### For Bathrooms

- **Marble & Limestone**: Classic choices for marble bathroom design, lending a spa-like atmosphere and timeless appeal.
- **Terrazzo**: A contemporary option, offering color versatility and a nod to mid-century modern aesthetics.

### For Living Spaces

- **Sandstone & Travertine**: Bring warmth and subtle texture to fireplaces, floors, and bespoke stone furniture.
- **Onyx**: For those seeking drama—backlit onyx creates a captivating focal point in lounges or home bars.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

StoneLab’s exclusive stone interiors are designed to stand the test of time. To maintain their pristine appearance:

- **Clean Gently**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid etching or scratching.
- **Seal Regularly**: Especially for marble and limestone, periodic sealing provides protection against stains.
- **Address Spills Promptly**: Wipe up liquids and oils as soon as possible to prevent absorption.
- **Avoid Abrasives**: Skip harsh chemicals and rough sponges to preserve the finish.
