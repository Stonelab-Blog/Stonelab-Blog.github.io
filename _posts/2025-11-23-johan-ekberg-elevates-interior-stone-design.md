## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Every Space in Luxury Homes and Developments

Choosing the right stone is essential for achieving both beauty and longevity. Here are expert tips for different spaces:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops. These materials resist stains and scratches, making them ideal for high-traffic luxury kitchens.
- **Bathroom:** Marble remains a classic for its refined look, perfect for marble bathroom design, but ensure it is sealed and maintained to protect against moisture.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture or accent walls crafted from limestone or travertine introduce organic warmth and texture.
- **Entryways:** Hardwearing stones such as slate or basalt create striking first impressions and withstand frequent use.

Collaborating with a specialist like StoneLab ensures each stone is meticulously selected and crafted for the space’s unique requirements, reflecting Swedish stone craftsmanship and tailored interior stone design expertise.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty of Stone Interiors

Stone surfaces reward attentive care. Maintain their pristine appearance with these simple guidelines:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid etching or dullness.
- **Prompt spill management:** Especially for marble, quickly wipe away acidic spills to prevent staining.
- **Sealing:** Periodically reseal surfaces, particularly in kitchens and baths, to preserve their protective barrier.
- **Avoid abrasive tools:** Gentle cleaning preserves the stone’s natural finish and integrity.
