### Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space

Choosing the perfect stone requires balancing aesthetic preferences with the practical demands of each room. StoneLab’s custom stone solutions are tailored to both the visual and functional needs of luxury spaces.

**Consider these expert recommendations:**

- **Kitchen stone countertops:** Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite that withstand daily use and resist staining.
- **Marble bathroom design:** Select high-quality marble with a sealed finish for a spa-like ambiance and effortless maintenance.
- **Living areas:** Incorporate bespoke stone furniture to create striking focal points that blend seamlessly with your décor.

### Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty

Stone surfaces are an investment in luxury, and with proper care, they will maintain their elegance for generations.

- **Clean regularly:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to preserve the stone’s finish.
- **Seal surfaces:** Periodic sealing protects against moisture and stains—especially important for marble and limestone.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals:** Select gentle products to prevent etching or dullness.
- **Address spills promptly:** Quick action prevents staining and keeps surfaces pristine.
