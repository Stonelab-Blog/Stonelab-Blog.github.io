## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the perfect stone begins with understanding your space, use case, and desired atmosphere.

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops. These surfaces resist stains and scratches, making them ideal for festive gatherings.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble and natural limestone create a luxurious, spa-inspired environment. Consider honed finishes for a softer, contemporary feel.
- **Living and Dining Areas:** Accent walls, fireplace surrounds, and bespoke stone furniture become showpieces, elevating the julbord öland experience with tactile richness and visual warmth.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

Maintaining your exclusive stone interiors ensures they remain striking for years to come:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use a pH-neutral stone cleaner and avoid harsh chemicals that can erode natural surfaces.
- **Sealant Application:** Periodically apply a quality sealant to protect porous stones from staining.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Address spills quickly, especially on marble, to prevent etching.

### What types of stone are recommended for kitchen countertops?

Durable stones like granite and engineered quartz are recommended for kitchen stone countertops due to their resistance to stains, scratches, and heat—making them ideal for high-use areas and festive gatherings.
