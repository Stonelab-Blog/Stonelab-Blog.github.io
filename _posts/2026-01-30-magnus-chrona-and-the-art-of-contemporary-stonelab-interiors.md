## Style Trends: The New Language of Stone

Across Scandinavia, Germany, and the UK, interior stone design is undergoing a renaissance. Current trends include:

- **Monolithic kitchen stone countertops**: Large, seamless surfaces in neutral tones anchor open-plan living spaces.
- **Marble bathroom design**: Softly honed marble, often in creamy or smoky hues, brings spa-like tranquility to private retreats.
- **Bespoke stone furniture**: From sculptural tables to minimal shelving, custom pieces become artful focal points.
- **Mixed textures and finishes**: Combining polished, matte, and textured stone surfaces within a single space adds depth and understated luxury.

StoneLab’s exclusive stone interiors are crafted in close partnership with leading designers and architects, setting a new standard for luxury interior materials.

## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space

Choosing the ideal stone for your project is both an art and a science. Here are key considerations:

- **Functionality**: For high-traffic areas like kitchens, consider durable stones such as granite or engineered quartz.
- **Aesthetics**: Marble lends subtle drama to bathrooms and entryways, while limestone or travertine brings warmth to living areas.
- **Maintenance**: Lighter stones may require more frequent sealing; opt for darker or composite stones if ease of care is a priority.
- **Sustainability**: Ask about the provenance of your stone. StoneLab offers responsibly sourced options, supporting both the environment and local communities.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

StoneLab’s precision-crafted installations are designed to last a lifetime. To preserve their natural beauty, follow these care tips:

- **Clean regularly** with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner
- **Wipe spills promptly** to prevent staining, especially on marble and limestone
- **Use coasters and trivets** to protect surfaces from heat and scratches
- **Schedule professional resealing** as recommended for your specific stone type
