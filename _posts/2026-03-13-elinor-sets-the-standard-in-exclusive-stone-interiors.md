## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the ideal stone is essential for both aesthetics and practicality. Consider the following expert tips:

- **Kitchens:** For kitchen stone countertops, opt for durable stones such as granite or quartzite, which offer resilience against heat and stains while adding an unmistakable air of luxury.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic choice. Its soft veining and luminous surface create a spa-like atmosphere. Consider honed finishes for a contemporary edge and improved slip resistance.
- **Living Spaces:** Incorporate bespoke stone furniture or accent walls to anchor open-plan living areas. Natural stone’s subtle variations ensure every piece is unique.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors, follow these maintenance guidelines:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to maintain the stone’s natural finish.
- **Sealing:** Periodic sealing protects porous stones such as marble and limestone from stains and moisture.
- **Preventative Care:** Promptly clean spills, use coasters and mats, and avoid harsh chemicals to ensure surfaces remain immaculate.
