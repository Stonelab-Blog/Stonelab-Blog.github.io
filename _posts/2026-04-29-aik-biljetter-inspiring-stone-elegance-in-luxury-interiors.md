## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Luxury Interiors

Choosing the ideal stone for your interiors requires balancing beauty, functionality, and sustainability. Here’s how to ensure you make the right selection:

- **Consider Usage:** High-traffic spaces like kitchens benefit from harder, more resistant stones such as granite or quartzite, while marble’s luxurious appeal is perfect for bathrooms and statement walls.
- **Texture and Finish:** Polished surfaces reflect light and add a modern touch, while honed or matte finishes create understated elegance.
- **Harmonize with Other Luxury Interior Materials:** Stone pairs beautifully with luxury interior materials such as wood, glass, and metals—layering these textures adds depth and interest.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Stone Surfaces

To keep your stone surfaces pristine for years to come, follow these expert recommendations:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to prevent buildup and protect the stone’s finish.
- **Seal Regularly:** Depending on the stone type, periodic sealing will help guard against staining and moisture penetration.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Quickly blot up spills, especially acidic substances, to avoid etching or discoloration.
