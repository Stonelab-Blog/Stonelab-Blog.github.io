## Choosing the Perfect Stone for Every Space

Selecting the right stone is essential for achieving a harmonious interior. Here are practical tips to guide your decision:

- **For kitchens**: Opt for dense, non-porous stones like granite or engineered quartz for countertops—these combine luxury with durability and easy maintenance.
- **For bathrooms**: Marble remains a classic for its elegance, but consider limestone or travertine for a softer, spa-like atmosphere.
- **For living areas**: Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or accent walls, adds a distinctive focal point and elevates the room.
- **For sustainability**: Ask about locally sourced or recycled stone options to reduce your project’s environmental footprint.

StoneLab’s expertise ensures each project matches your vision while maximizing the natural qualities of every slab.

## Maintaining the Beauty of Stone Surfaces

To preserve the allure of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Clean regularly** with pH-neutral, stone-safe cleaners.
- **Avoid acidic substances** (like citrus or vinegar) to protect finishes, especially on marble.
- **Seal porous stones** annually to prevent staining and moisture damage.
- **Use coasters and mats** on stone surfaces to guard against scratches and heat marks.

StoneLab provides guidance on care and maintenance, ensuring your custom stone solutions remain timeless investments.
