## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space: Expert Interior Stone Design Advice

Choosing the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Consider these expert guidelines:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for countertops—both resistant to heat and daily wear.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble or limestone offer an elegant finish, but require regular sealing to maintain their pristine appearance.
- **Living areas**: For feature walls or floors, consider travertine or terrazzo for a contemporary, inviting feel.
- **Furniture**: Bespoke stone furniture in onyx or soapstone offers unique coloration and texture, ensuring a one-of-a-kind statement.

Consulting with StoneLab’s design specialists ensures that your selection not only meets aesthetic expectations but also fits the practical demands of your lifestyle.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces: Maintenance Tips for Luxury Interior Materials

To keep your stone looking its best for years to come, follow these maintenance tips:

- Wipe spills immediately to prevent staining, especially on marble and limestone.
- Use pH-neutral cleaning products designed specifically for stone.
- Reseal porous stones annually to maintain their resistance to moisture and etching.
- Protect surfaces with coasters, mats, and felt pads under heavy objects.
