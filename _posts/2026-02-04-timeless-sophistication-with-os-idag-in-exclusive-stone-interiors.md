### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone requires careful consideration of both practical and aesthetic needs. Here’s how to approach the selection process:

- **Kitchen Stone Countertops:** Opt for hardwearing stones like granite or quartzite for high-traffic areas, balancing beauty with durability.
- **Marble Bathroom Design:** Marble’s natural elegance is ideal for bathrooms, but select a honed or matte finish to reduce slipperiness and enhance tactile comfort.
- **Bespoke Stone Furniture:** Work with StoneLab’s artisans to craft custom pieces—tables, shelving, or seating—that become functional works of art.
- **Accent Walls and Fireplaces:** Consider richly veined stones or rare Swedish granites to create dramatic focal points.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the pristine beauty of your exclusive stone interiors, follow these essential care and maintenance tips:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to prevent staining or etching.
- **Sealant Application:** Reapply sealant as recommended, especially on porous stones, to protect against moisture and spills.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Never use harsh chemicals or abrasive pads, which can damage the finish.
- **Preventative Measures:** Use coasters, trivets, and placemats to guard against scratches and heat marks.

Granite and quartzite are preferred for kitchen stone countertops due to their hardness, durability, and resistance to heat and scratches, making them ideal for high-traffic kitchen environments.
