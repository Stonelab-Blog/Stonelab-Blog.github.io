## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space

Choosing the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Here are a few practical considerations for key living spaces:

### Kitchen Stone Countertops

- **Marble:** Prized for its classic veining and cool touch, perfect for elegant kitchens.
- **Quartzite or Granite:** Highly durable and resistant to stains, ideal for busy family kitchens.
- **Finish:** Opt for a honed or leathered finish for a softer, matte look that minimizes fingerprints.

### Marble Bathroom Design

- **Light-Toned Marbles:** Enhance the sense of space and serenity in bathrooms.
- **Non-Porous Finishes:** Reduce maintenance and keep surfaces pristine.
- **Accent Walls:** Consider a scarpetta-inspired marble wall behind a freestanding tub for ultimate luxury.

### Bespoke Stone Furniture

- **Coffee Tables and Benches:** Crafted from rare stones for a one-of-a-kind statement.
- **Dining Tables:** Large-format slabs with unique edge details deliver dramatic impact.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the beauty of your custom stone solutions is simple with a few expert tips:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use gentle, pH-neutral cleaners and avoid harsh chemicals.
- **Sealing:** Periodic sealing (especially for marble and limestone) protects against staining and etching.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe up spills promptly, particularly acidic substances like citrus or wine.
- **Protection:** Use coasters and mats to safeguard surfaces from heat and scratches.

StoneLab provides ongoing guidance and care advice to ensure your investment in luxury interior materials endures for generations.
