## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the beauty and integrity of stone surfaces requires a thoughtful approach:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and a soft cloth to preserve the stone’s finish.
- **Sealing:** Periodically seal porous stones like marble and limestone to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Immediate attention:** Wipe up spills promptly, particularly acidic substances, to prevent etching or discoloration.
- **Avoid abrasive products:** Protect the surface by steering clear of harsh chemicals or scouring pads.
