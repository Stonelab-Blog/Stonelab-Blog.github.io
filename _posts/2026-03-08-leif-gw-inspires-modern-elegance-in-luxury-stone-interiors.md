- **Sustainable stone sourcing:** Designers and clients are increasingly seeking out materials with transparent provenance and eco-friendly credentials—an area where StoneLab leads with pride.
- **Statement stone furniture:** Bespoke stone pieces, such as sculptural coffee tables or kitchen islands, are redefining what luxury furniture can be.
- **Contrasts and combinations:** Pairing cool Scandinavian stones with warm woods or metals brings dynamic visual interest to exclusive stone interiors.

## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space in Sweden, Germany, and the UK

Choosing the ideal stone is an art in itself. Consider these practical guidelines:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable and non-porous materials like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops. These stones resist stains and scratches while exuding understated elegance.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a hallmark of luxury. Select high-quality marble with protective treatments for lasting beauty and a spa-like atmosphere.
- **Living Areas:** For statement pieces or wall cladding, explore unique Swedish stones with distinctive colors or patterns that reflect the local landscape and heritage.
- **Furniture:** Bespoke stone furniture demands precision and imagination. Work with experts to select stones that balance weight, strength, and aesthetic appeal.

## Caring for Stone Surfaces: Maintaining Luxury Stone Interiors

Maintaining the pristine look of your stone interiors requires simple, regular care:

- **Daily cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to remove dust and spills.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals:** Acidic or abrasive cleaners can damage the finish and texture.
- **Seal regularly:** Natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to protect against moisture and staining, especially in kitchens and bathrooms.
- **Address spills promptly:** Quickly blot and clean to minimize the risk of permanent marks, particularly with marble and lighter stones.
