## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone is an art in itself. Here are expert tips to guide your selection:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for hardwearing stones like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops, balancing beauty with resilience against daily wear.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic, infusing spaces with light and luxury. For spa-like tranquility, consider soft-toned travertine or limestone.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture or feature walls in striking marble or onyx can transform a living room into a gallery-worthy retreat.
- **Entrances and Hallways:** Make a statement with large-format stones or intricate inlays, setting the tone for the entire home.

## Caring for Your StoneLab Surfaces

To maintain the allure of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth with mild, pH-neutral cleaners to avoid etching or dullness.
- **Seal Regularly:** Periodic sealing protects against stains, especially on more porous stones.
- **Preventive Measures:** Use coasters, trivets, and mats under hot or acidic items to preserve surface integrity.
- **Professional Care:** For deep cleaning or restoration, trust in professional services familiar with luxury interior materials.
