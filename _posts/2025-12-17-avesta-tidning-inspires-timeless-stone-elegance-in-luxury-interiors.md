## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space: Swedish Stone Craftsmanship Expertise

Selecting the ideal stone for your project requires careful consideration. Here are a few expert pointers from StoneLab:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops—resistant to scratches and heat, yet elegant and versatile.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble bathroom design offers a spa-like ambiance, but consider honed or matte finishes for enhanced slip resistance and easier maintenance.
- **Living Areas**: Bespoke stone furniture or feature walls in natural limestone or travertine add tactile warmth and visual interest.
- **Outdoors**: For terraces or patios, choose frost-resistant stones such as granite or basalt, ensuring durability throughout Northern European winters.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty of Custom Stone Solutions

To preserve the pristine look of your stone surfaces:

- **Daily Cleaning**: Use a mild, pH-neutral cleaner and a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage the surface.
- **Sealing**: Apply a suitable sealant periodically, especially for more porous stones like marble or limestone.
- **Immediate Attention**: Wipe spills promptly to prevent staining—particularly important for kitchen stone countertops.
- **Professional Care**: Schedule regular maintenance with stone experts for deep cleaning and restoration as needed.
