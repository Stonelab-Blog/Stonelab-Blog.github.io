## How to Select the Right Stone for Every Space in Luxury Interior Design

Choosing the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Here’s how to make a confident choice:

### For Kitchens

- **Best for:** Quartzite, granite, and select marbles.
- **Why:** These stones offer resilience against stains and scratches, making them ideal for kitchen stone countertops.
- **Tip:** Opt for neutral or earth-toned stones that complement both modern and traditional cabinetry.

### For Bathrooms

- **Best for:** Marble and limestone.
- **Why:** Their natural veining enhances the serene feel of a luxury bathroom. Marble bathroom design remains a signature of high-end interiors.
- **Tip:** Choose stones with honed finishes for a softer, non-slip surface.

### For Living Spaces

- **Best for:** Travertine, terrazzo, and custom inlays.
- **Why:** These materials create striking floors or statement walls, reflecting the exclusivity of StoneLab’s interior stone design.
- **Tip:** Consider bespoke stone furniture for a cohesive, curated look.

## Stone Care and Maintenance Essentials for Exclusive Stone Interiors

Luxury materials deserve conscientious care. Preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors with these guidelines:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damaging the surface.
- **Sealant application:** Periodically reseal kitchen stone countertops and bathroom surfaces to protect against stains.
- **Immediate attention:** Wipe spills quickly, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus.
