## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone depends on your project’s function and mood:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for granite or quartzite for countertops—these stones offer exceptional durability and timeless appeal.  
- **Bathrooms:** Marble or travertine delivers a soft, luminous effect, perfect for creating tranquil retreats.  
- **Living Areas:** Consider limestone or sandstone for feature walls and bespoke furniture, lending warmth and understated luxury.  
- **Entryways:** Durable and striking stones like slate or basalt withstand heavy use while making a memorable first impression.

Our StoneLab experts guide clients through selecting the most suitable **luxury interior materials**, ensuring every project is both beautiful and practical.

## Care and Maintenance Tips

To keep your stone surfaces pristine:

- Clean regularly with a mild pH-neutral cleaner—avoid harsh chemicals  
- Wipe spills promptly, especially on marble, to prevent staining  
- Seal surfaces as recommended to enhance longevity and resist moisture  
- Use coasters and trivets to protect from heat and scratching  
- Dust and polish bespoke stone furniture to maintain its natural sheen

With the right care, your custom stone solutions will retain their elegance for generations.
