### Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Each space in the home demands a thoughtful approach to material, finish, and function.

- **Kitchen:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops, balancing beauty with resilience against daily wear.
- **Bathroom:** For marble bathroom design, consider honed finishes for slip resistance and a soft, contemporary look.
- **Living Areas:** Embrace limestone or travertine for statement walls or bespoke stone furniture, bringing warmth and organic character.
- **Entryways:** Swedish stone craftsmanship shines in high-traffic areas, where robust stones like slate or sandstone stand the test of time.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the investment and beauty of your stone surfaces, regular care is essential:

- **Seal regularly:** Especially for porous stones like marble and limestone.
- **Clean gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and avoid harsh chemicals.
- **Protect surfaces:** Use coasters and mats to prevent staining.
- **Address spills promptly:** Especially acidic substances on marble or travertine.

StoneLab offers guidance on optimal care routines, ensuring your surfaces remain pristine and luxurious for years to come.
