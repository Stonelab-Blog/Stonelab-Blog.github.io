### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Sweden and Northern Europe

Choosing the right stone requires a balance of function, beauty, and environmental responsibility. StoneLab’s expertise in interior stone design ensures that each project is tailored to the unique vision of our clients across Sweden, Scandinavia, Northern Europe, Germany, and the UK.

**Consider these factors when selecting stone for your project:**

- **Purpose of the space:** For high-traffic areas like kitchens, opt for durable stones such as granite or engineered quartz. For spaces seeking a touch of opulence, marble offers unmatched elegance.
- **Colour and texture:** Light-coloured stones can make smaller rooms feel spacious, while darker tones add drama and depth. Honed finishes suit modern interiors, while polished surfaces bring out the stone’s natural luster.
- **Sourcing and sustainability:** Prioritize luxury interior materials from suppliers committed to ethical quarrying and minimal environmental impact.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Luxury stone deserves thoughtful maintenance to preserve its striking appearance:

- **Daily cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to avoid damaging the surface.
- **Sealing:** Regularly seal porous stones like marble to prevent staining.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals:** Stay clear of acidic or abrasive cleaners that can etch or scratch the stone.
- **Prompt spill management:** Especially important for kitchen stone countertops and marble bathroom design, as oils and acids can quickly penetrate untreated surfaces.
