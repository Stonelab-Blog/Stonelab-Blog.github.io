#### Current Trends in Interior Stone Design

Reflecting the discerning tastes of luxury homeowners and design professionals, the current trends in interior stone design emphasize:

- **Minimalist Luxury:** Clean lines, subtle textures, and a restrained palette of natural hues define today’s most sought-after spaces. Large-format stone slabs and seamless finishes are particularly popular, offering both visual impact and a feeling of effortless elegance.
- **Sustainable Stone Sourcing:** Clients are increasingly prioritizing environmentally-friendly options. StoneLab is proud to champion sustainable sourcing, ensuring our materials are responsibly quarried and processed with minimal environmental impact.
- **Bespoke Stone Furniture:** Custom stone tables, shelving, and even lighting fixtures are making a statement in contemporary interiors, blending artistry with functionality.
- **Contrasting Finishes:** Combining polished and honed surfaces within a single space adds depth and tactile interest, perfect for high-end kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas.

### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone requires careful consideration of both aesthetics and performance. Here are some expert tips from StoneLab:

- **Kitchens:** For kitchen stone countertops, durability and stain resistance are paramount. Granite and engineered quartz offer robust options, while marble adds unrivalled luxury—ideal for less high-traffic zones or feature islands.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a favorite for its opulent look and smooth feel. Pair with matte finishes or subtle veining for a modern twist.
- **Living Areas:** For statement walls or bespoke stone furniture, consider the interplay of color, grain, and texture. Limestone and travertine deliver warmth, while onyx and rare marbles provide dramatic focal points.
- **Outdoor Spaces:** Select stones with proven resilience to Northern European climates, such as granite or certain sandstones, ensuring both beauty and longevity.

### Care and Maintenance for Enduring Beauty

Proper care extends the life and luster of your stone surfaces. StoneLab recommends:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to protect the stone’s finish.
- **Sealing:** Periodic sealing defends against moisture and stains, especially for kitchen and bathroom installations.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Wipe up spills immediately, particularly acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching.
- **Professional Restoration:** For scratches or dullness, professional polishing restores the stone’s original brilliance.

### Which types of stone are best suited for kitchen countertops?

For kitchen stone countertops, StoneLab recommends granite and engineered quartz for their durability and stain resistance, while marble is ideal for less trafficked areas or statement kitchen islands due to its luxurious appearance.
