### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Luxury Interior Project

Choosing the ideal stone for your interior is both an art and a science. Here are some expert tips from the StoneLab team for luxury homeowners, interior designers, and architects:

- **Consider the function:** For high-traffic areas such as kitchens, granite or quartzite offer lasting durability. In serene spaces like bathrooms, marble brings a sense of calm and luxury.
- **Embrace individuality:** Brott surfaces celebrate the unique characteristics of each stone slab. Select pieces with natural veining and organic edges to make a bold, personalized statement.
- **Coordinate with your design vision:** Pair cool-toned stones with minimalist, Scandinavian-inspired interiors, or select warmer hues for a more inviting, classic atmosphere.

### Maintenance and Care for Enduring Beauty of Your Stone Interiors

Preserving the elegance of your custom stone solutions is simple with a few expert strategies:

- **Clean regularly with mild, pH-neutral products** to avoid damaging the surface.
- **Wipe spills promptly** to prevent staining, especially on lighter stones such as marble.
- **Seal surfaces periodically** to enhance stain resistance and maintain the stone’s natural lustre.
- **Use soft cloths or sponges** to avoid scratching.

By following these guidelines, your luxury interior materials retain their brilliance and integrity for years to come.
