#### Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

Premium stone surfaces are investments in luxury and longevity. With thoughtful maintenance, they will retain their allure for generations.

- **Daily Care:** Wipe surfaces with a soft, damp cloth and use pH-neutral cleaners.
- **Protection:** Apply sealant annually, especially on marble and limestone, to guard against stains.
- **Immediate Attention:** Clean spills promptly to prevent etching or discolouration, particularly with acidic substances like wine or citrus.

StoneLab provides tailored care guidance for every installation, ensuring your exclusive stone interiors remain pristine.
