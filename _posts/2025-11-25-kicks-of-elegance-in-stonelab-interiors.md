## Style Trends: Statement Stone “Kicks”

Modern luxury interiors are embracing bold stone elements—think striking kitchen islands, sculptural bespoke stone furniture, or dramatic stone feature walls. These “kicks” of stone serve as focal points, infusing spaces with personality and refined texture. Some current trends include:

- **Monolithic Islands:** Large-scale kitchen stone countertops in rich marbles or sleek granites, acting as the heart of the home.
- **Integrated Stone Lighting:** Seamless stone surfaces with embedded lighting for a soft, ambient glow.
- **Contrasting Finishes:** Pairing polished and honed surfaces to create depth and movement in exclusive stone interiors.
- **Sculptural Details:** Custom-carved elements that transform functional pieces—like sinks or shelving—into art.

## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Every Space

Choosing the right stone is essential for both aesthetics and performance. Here’s how to make the most of your options:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable, low-porosity stones such as quartzite or granite for countertops. These materials withstand daily use while maintaining their luxurious appearance.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble is a classic choice for bathroom design, lending a spa-like feel. For added practicality, consider honed finishes that resist water spots and fingerprints.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture—coffee tables, sideboards, or fireplaces—acts as a striking centerpiece.
- **Accent Walls and Floors:** Use richly veined stones to create visual drama, especially in entryways or living rooms.

Collaborate with professionals like StoneLab to navigate the nuances of stone selection, ensuring the final result aligns with your vision and lifestyle.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors is simple with the right care:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use a pH-neutral cleaner and a soft cloth to avoid etching or scratching.
- **Seal Regularly:** Many natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to protect against stains.
- **Address Spills Promptly:** Especially with materials like marble, wipe up spills to prevent staining.
- **Use Mats and Trivets:** Protect stone surfaces in kitchens and bathrooms from heat and heavy impacts.

By following these tips, your stone elements will continue to impress for years to come.
