## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Every Space in Northern Europe

Choosing the right stone is both an art and a science. Here are some practical guidelines:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable, low-porosity stones such as granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops. These surfaces offer both resilience and refined aesthetics.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble bathroom design remains a classic choice. Its veining and natural character create a spa-like atmosphere, but be sure to select appropriate sealants to protect against moisture.
- **Living Spaces**: For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, consider stones with unique patterns to serve as conversation pieces while harmonizing with the overall design.
- **Entryways and Staircases**: Hard-wearing stones like limestone or slate deliver elegance and withstand high foot traffic.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty of Stone Interiors

Even the most exclusive stone interiors require thoughtful maintenance:

- **Daily Cleaning**: Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to protect the surface’s integrity.
- **Sealing**: Apply sealants regularly to prevent staining and enhance longevity, especially for marble and limestone.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals**: Steer clear of acidic or abrasive cleaners, which can etch or dull the finish.
- **Prompt Spill Management**: Wipe up spills quickly to prevent stains, particularly on lighter stones.

By adhering to these care tips, you’ll preserve the luxury and integrity of your stone surfaces for generations.
