## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space in Luxury Interiors

Choosing the ideal stone for your project is both an art and a science. Consider the following advice from StoneLab’s experts:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for dense, non-porous stones such as granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops. These materials offer superior resistance to staining and scratching—perfect for high-use areas.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble remains a favorite for its luxurious veining and luminous finish. For wet areas, ensure professional sealing to preserve its beauty.
- **Living Areas**: Softer stones like travertine or limestone can add warmth to living rooms and entryways, especially when used for flooring or accent walls.
- **Furniture**: Bespoke stone furniture in onyx or rare marbles becomes the centrepiece of any room, combining functionality with artistic flair.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Stone Surfaces from StoneLab

StoneLab’s custom stone solutions are designed for longevity, but proper care will keep them stunning for years to come:

- **Routine Cleaning**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid surface damage.
- **Sealing**: Regularly seal marble and other porous stones to protect against moisture and staining.
- **Avoid Abrasives**: Never use harsh chemicals or scouring pads, which can erode finishes and dull the stone’s natural shine.
- **Prompt Attention**: Wipe spills immediately, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching or discoloration.
