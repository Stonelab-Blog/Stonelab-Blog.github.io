## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Luxury Interiors

Choosing the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Here are StoneLab’s expert tips for selecting luxury interior materials:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for durable surfaces like granite or quartzite for countertops, balancing beauty with resilience.  
- **Bathroom:** Marble is a classic choice for elegance, but consider slip-resistant finishes for safety.  
- **Living Areas:** Limestone and sandstone offer warmth and subtle texture, perfect for feature walls or bespoke furniture.  
- **Accent Pieces:** Use dramatic stones—such as Swedish green marble—for statement tables or shelving.

Our team guides clients through every decision, ensuring the chosen stone matches your aesthetic, functional needs, and sustainability values.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

With proper care, StoneLab’s custom stone solutions will retain their beauty for generations. Keep your exclusive stone interiors pristine with these simple tips:

- **Clean regularly** with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner  
- **Avoid harsh chemicals** that can dull or etch surfaces  
- **Seal porous stones** (like marble and limestone) annually to prevent staining  
- **Promptly wipe spills**, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus

If you ever need personalized advice, StoneLab’s aftercare specialists are always available to assist.
