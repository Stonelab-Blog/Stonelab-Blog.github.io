## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space

Choosing the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Consider these practical tips:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops—these materials resist stains and scratches, maintaining their beauty under daily use.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design is a classic choice, but ensure proper sealing to protect against moisture and etching.
- **Living Spaces:** Limestone and travertine provide soft, elegant tones for living rooms or bespoke stone furniture.
- **Accent Features:** Statement stones like onyx or rare marbles create unforgettable focal points in entryways or lounges.

StoneLab’s designers guide clients through every step, ensuring that each selection reflects the unique vision of the homeowner or architect.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Stone Surfaces

To preserve the sophistication of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Seal regularly:** Especially for porous stones like marble and limestone.
- **Clean gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths; avoid harsh chemicals.
- **Wipe spills promptly:** Particularly acidic substances like wine or citrus.
- **Use coasters and mats:** Protect surfaces from heat and heavy objects.

With proper care, your custom stone solutions will remain pristine and luxurious for generations.
