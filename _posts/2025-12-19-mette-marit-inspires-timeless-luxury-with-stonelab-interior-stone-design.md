- **Sustainable Stone Sourcing:** Increasingly, designers are asking for environmentally responsible options—an area where StoneLab leads with traceable, sustainably quarried materials.

### Expert Advice for Selecting the Right Stone for Luxury Interior Materials

Choosing the perfect stone is both an art and a science. StoneLab’s team offers tailored guidance to ensure your investment stands the test of time:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable granites or engineered quartz for high-traffic kitchen stone countertops. For a luxurious statement, consider a bold-veined marble as a showpiece.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a hallmark of luxury. Select stones with low porosity and proper sealing to ensure long-lasting beauty.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or sideboards, introduces an element of sophistication without overwhelming the room.
- **Accent Walls & Fireplaces:** Travertine or slate can add warmth and texture, complementing both modern and traditional interiors.

### Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty in Exclusive Stone Interiors

StoneLab’s custom stone solutions are designed to be enjoyed for generations. With proper care, your exclusive stone interiors will maintain their elegance year after year:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to avoid etching or dulling the surface.
- **Sealing:** High-quality sealants protect surfaces from stains and moisture—especially important for marble bathroom design and kitchen stone countertops.
- **Preventative Measures:** Use coasters, trivets, and felt pads on bespoke stone furniture to prevent scratches and marks.
- **Regular Maintenance:** Schedule periodic professional cleaning and resealing to preserve the depth of color and luster.
