## Style Trends in Modern Emporia Design Across Northern Europe

Contemporary emporia interiors blend classic grandeur with modern minimalism. Here are some current trends shaping the niche:

- **Monolithic Stone Surfaces:** Large-format stone—used for floors, wall panels, and kitchen islands—creates a seamless, gallery-like effect.
- **Soft, Natural Palettes:** Scandinavian-inspired whites, greys, and earth tones dominate, letting the stone’s natural veining take center stage.
- **Integrated Lighting:** Discreet lighting accentuates the unique textures and patterns of interior stone design, adding drama and sophistication.
- **Sculptural Stone Furniture:** Bespoke stone furniture pieces serve as functional art, defining spaces with their presence.

## Choosing the Perfect Stone for Each Space in Your Luxury Home

Selecting the right stone for your luxury interior is both an art and a science. Here are a few considerations from StoneLab’s design experts:

- **Kitchen Stone Countertops:** Opt for granite or quartzite for unrivaled durability in high-traffic kitchens. Marble offers unrivaled beauty for statement islands and accent pieces.
- **Marble Bathroom Design:** Marble exudes elegance in bathrooms, especially when paired with underfloor heating for comfort.
- **Bespoke Stone Furniture:** Choose stones with unique veining or rare coloration to create one-of-a-kind tables, benches, and shelving.
- **Consistency and Contrast:** For open-plan spaces, consider using the same stone in different finishes to unify the design while adding subtle contrast.

## Caring for Your StoneLab Surfaces in Sweden and Beyond

Maintaining the beauty of your custom stone solutions is simple with these care tips:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use a pH-neutral cleaner and a soft cloth to preserve the stone’s finish.
- **Seal Regularly:** Periodic sealing protects against staining and keeps surfaces looking pristine.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals and abrasive pads that could damage the stone.
- **Attend to Spills:** Promptly wipe up spills, especially from acidic substances, to prevent etching or discoloration.
