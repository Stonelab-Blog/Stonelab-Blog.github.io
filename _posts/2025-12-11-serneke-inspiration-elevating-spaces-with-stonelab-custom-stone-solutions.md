### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Sweden, Germany, and the UK

Choosing the perfect stone involves balancing practicality with personal taste. Here are StoneLab’s expert tips for selecting exclusive stone interiors tailored to specific environments:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for hard-wearing granites or quartzites for countertops—these resist stains and scratches, making them ideal for high-traffic, functional luxury kitchen stone countertops.
- **Bathroom:** Marble or limestone creates a calm, spa-like ambiance. For added sustainability, consider locally sourced options that reduce transportation impact.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture and accent walls in travertine or onyx bring warmth and sophistication without overwhelming the room’s design.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces to Preserve Luxury Interior Materials

To preserve the beauty of your investment, StoneLab recommends the following care regimen:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral, stone-safe cleaners to avoid etching and discoloration.
- **Sealing:** Apply a high-quality stone sealer annually to maintain resistance to moisture and stains.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe spills promptly, especially with acidic substances, to prevent permanent marks.
- **Gentle Tools:** Avoid abrasive pads or harsh chemicals; microfibre cloths are ideal.
