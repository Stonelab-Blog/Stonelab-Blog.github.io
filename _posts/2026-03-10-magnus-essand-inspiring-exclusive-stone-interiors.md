## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Luxury Homes and Projects

Selecting the ideal stone requires balancing aesthetics, durability, and daily use. Consider the following:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for granite or engineered stone for kitchen stone countertops, prized for their resistance to heat, scratches, and stains.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic, but newer treatments increase stain resistance and longevity.
- **Living Areas:** Limestone and travertine bring warmth and elegance to larger spaces, while bespoke stone furniture adds a personal touch.
- **Entryways:** Hard-wearing stones like basalt or quartzite create lasting first impressions.

Our team at StoneLab works closely with clients in Sweden, Germany, and the UK to guide material selection, ensuring each stone is perfectly suited to its intended purpose.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors, follow these expert tips:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damaging the surface.
- **Sealing:** Natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals and abrasive pads, which can dull or scratch stone surfaces.
- **Prompt Spill Response:** Especially for marble, clean spills quickly to prevent etching or discoloration.

With proper care, the luxury and elegance of your stonework will remain undiminished for years to come.
