### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone begins with understanding both function and aesthetic vision. Here’s how to approach selection for different spaces:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust stones like granite or quartzite for countertops. These offer superior resistance to heat and scratching, ideal for high-traffic areas.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a favorite for its luminous beauty. For a contemporary twist, consider large-format marble tiles or seamless stone sinks.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or shelving, brings natural elegance and individuality to communal spaces.
- **Entryways:** Durable, textured stones make for striking first impressions while withstanding daily wear.

StoneLab consultants work collaboratively with architects, interior designers, and homeowners to source and craft tailored solutions that embody exclusivity and function.

### Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

To maintain the pristine appearance of your custom stone solutions:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid dulling the finish.
- **Seal surfaces:** Periodic sealing protects against staining and moisture, especially in kitchens and bathrooms.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic products which can etch or discolor natural stone.
- **Prompt attention to spills:** Blot, don’t wipe, to prevent spreading and ensure a flawless surface.

StoneLab provides comprehensive aftercare guidance, empowering clients to preserve the integrity and allure of their investment.
