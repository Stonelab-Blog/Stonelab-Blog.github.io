### Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space in Luxury Interiors

Choosing the ideal stone begins with understanding each room’s function and character:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for hardwearing, non-porous stones like granite or quartzite for countertops. These provide both visual impact and daily practicality.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble and limestone introduce spa-like serenity. Their cool touch and elegant veining turn ordinary bathrooms into retreats.
- **Living Spaces:** Statement pieces crafted from locally sourced stone—like side tables or mantels—bring tactile luxury and timeless style.

Consulting with the StoneLab team ensures every stone is tailored in color, finish, and scale to your unique vision.

### Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty of Stone Interiors

Premium stone surfaces, once installed, reward regular yet simple care:
- Wipe surfaces with a soft, damp cloth and pH-neutral cleaner.
- Use coasters and trivets to protect from heat or acidic spills.
- Reseal marble and lighter stones periodically to maintain luster and resilience.

StoneLab provides ongoing support and guidance so your interiors remain as stunning as the day they were installed.
