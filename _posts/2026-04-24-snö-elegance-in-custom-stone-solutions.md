## Style Trends Shaped by Snö in Northern Europe and the UK

The current trend leans into the soft whites, cool greys, and gentle veining found in marble bathroom designs and exclusive stone interiors. The snö-inspired palette pairs beautifully with both modern and classic décor styles prevalent in Scandinavian, German, and British homes.

**Trending applications:**

- **Marble bathroom design:** Crisp white marbles with subtle grey veining, reminiscent of freshly fallen snow, create serene retreats.
- **Kitchen stone countertops:** Polished white quartzites and granites reflect light, brightening spaces and adding understated luxury.
- **Bespoke stone furniture:** Custom-made tables, benches, and shelving pieces in snowy hues provide functional art for living spaces.

## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space with Sustainable Stone Sourcing

Choosing the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here’s what to consider:

- **Function:** High-traffic areas such as kitchens benefit from durable materials like granite or engineered quartz, while bathrooms can celebrate the luxury of marble.
- **Aesthetics:** Seek stones with the color and veining that align with your vision—whether you desire the pure white of snö or the layered textures of Nordic landscapes.
- **Sustainability:** Prioritize sustainable stone sourcing; StoneLab partners with responsible quarries to ensure minimal environmental impact and ethical practices.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Luxury Interior Materials

To preserve the pristine beauty of your stone surfaces:

- **Sealing:** Regularly seal porous stones to protect against staining and moisture.
- **Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral, non-abrasive cleaners to maintain the surface’s natural luster.
- **Avoiding Damage:** Place trivets under hot cookware and coasters beneath drinks to prevent etching and discoloration.
