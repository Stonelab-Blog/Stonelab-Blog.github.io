### Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Each room has its own requirements. Here’s how to approach stone selection for different interior applications:

- **Kitchen Countertops:** Opt for robust, low-porosity stones such as quartzite or granite. These surfaces handle daily use with grace and retain their sheen for years.
- **Bathroom Design:** Marble remains a classic for its luminous quality; however, sealants and regular care are essential to maintain its pristine appearance in humid environments.
- **Living Areas:** For statement floors or walls, consider large-format slabs with unique veining for a dramatic yet timeless appeal.
- **Bespoke Furniture:** Select stones with distinctive patterns or rare hues for one-of-a-kind pieces that serve as functional art.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty and performance of your exclusive stone interiors, follow these maintenance tips:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to prevent dullness and staining.
- **Sealing:** Regularly seal surfaces in high-traffic or moisture-prone areas to maintain resistance against spills and etching.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Never use harsh chemicals or rough sponges, as these can damage the finish and integrity of the stone.
- **Prompt Attention:** Wipe spills immediately, especially those from acidic substances like wine or citrus.
