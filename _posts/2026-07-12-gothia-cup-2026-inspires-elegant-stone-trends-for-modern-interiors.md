## Practical Advice for Selecting Stone in Luxury Interiors

Choosing the perfect stone for your space requires careful consideration of both aesthetics and functionality. Here are StoneLab’s expert recommendations:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable materials such as granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops. These offer resistance to heat, stains, and scratches while retaining a luxurious look.
- **Bathrooms:** For marble bathroom design, select moisture-resistant stones and ensure proper sealing to preserve their pristine finish.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture in travertine or limestone can introduce organic texture and sculptural interest, complementing both contemporary and classic interiors.
- **Accent Features:** Use onyx or colored marble to create unique statement pieces, from coffee tables to wall cladding.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Exclusive Stone Interiors

To keep your exclusive stone interiors looking impeccable:

- **Seal Regularly:** Natural stones require periodic sealing to protect against spills and stains—especially in kitchens and baths.
- **Clean Gently:** Use pH-neutral cleansers and soft cloths; avoid harsh chemicals that can erode the surface.
- **Address Spills Promptly:** Wipe up liquids immediately to prevent etching or discoloration.
- **Professional Restoration:** For deep cleaning or repairs, trust specialists experienced in Swedish stone craftsmanship.
