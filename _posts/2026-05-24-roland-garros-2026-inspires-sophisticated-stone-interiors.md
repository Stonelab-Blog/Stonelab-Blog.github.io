## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Luxury Interior in Scandinavia and Beyond

Choosing the ideal stone involves considering aesthetics, application, and performance. Here are practical tips to guide your selection:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for hard-wearing stones like granite or quartzite for countertops, balancing beauty with resilience.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design exudes spa-like luxury but benefits from expert sealing and care.
- **Living Areas:** Limestone and travertine add warmth to living spaces and work beautifully for bespoke stone furniture or flooring.
- **Accent Features:** Use rare or patterned stones as statement walls or fireplace surrounds to introduce drama and uniqueness.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty of Custom Stone Solutions

Stone surfaces are investments in artistry and longevity. With the right care, their beauty only deepens with time:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use a pH-neutral cleaner and avoid harsh chemicals to protect the stone’s finish.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Wipe up spills quickly, especially acidic substances, to prevent staining.
- **Periodic Sealing:** Many natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to maintain resistance to moisture and stains.
- **Gentle Handling:** Avoid abrasive pads and always use coasters or trivets on stone surfaces.
