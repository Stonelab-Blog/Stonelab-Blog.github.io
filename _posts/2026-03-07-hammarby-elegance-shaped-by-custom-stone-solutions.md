### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the ideal stone starts with understanding the unique needs of each environment:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable surfaces such as granite or quartzite for countertops, balancing practicality with luxurious appeal.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a favored choice for its luminous finish, perfect for spa-inspired retreats.
- **Living Areas:** Limestone and travertine add warmth and texture, ideal for statement walls or bespoke stone furniture.

StoneLab’s design consultants work closely with clients to select stones that complement existing interiors, ensuring every installation is both beautiful and functional.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the elegance of your stone interiors:

- Use pH-neutral cleaners to avoid etching and staining.
- Seal surfaces periodically, especially in high-traffic areas like kitchens and bathrooms.
- Wipe spills promptly to maintain the stone’s pristine finish.
- Avoid abrasive tools that can scratch the surface.

With proper care, your investment in StoneLab’s exclusive stone interiors will endure for generations.
