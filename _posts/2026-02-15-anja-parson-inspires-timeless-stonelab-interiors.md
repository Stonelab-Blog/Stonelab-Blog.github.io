## How to Select the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone for your project is an art. Here’s how to make the right selection for your unique vision:

### Kitchen Stone Countertops

- **Material:** Opt for granite or quartzite for durability and stain resistance.
- **Finish:** Honed finishes offer a soft, matte feel, while polished surfaces provide a striking sheen.
- **Colour:** Neutral tones, such as creamy whites and deep greys, complement modern Scandinavian aesthetics.

### Marble Bathroom Design

- **Material:** Marble remains a timeless choice for bathrooms, exuding spa-like serenity.
- **Texture:** Consider subtle veining for a tranquil ambience, or go bold with statement slabs for drama.
- **Practicality:** Ensure proper sealing to maintain lasting beauty in high-moisture settings.

### Bespoke Stone Furniture

- **Flexibility:** StoneLab crafts custom pieces to suit any vision, from minimalist consoles to sculptural dining tables.
- **Integration:** Match furniture stonework with existing interior stone design to create a harmonious flow.

## Caring for Your Exclusive Stone Interiors

Luxury interior materials deserve thoughtful care. To preserve the natural beauty and longevity of your stone surfaces:

- **Clean with mild, pH-neutral solutions** to avoid etching or staining.
- **Use coasters and trivets** on kitchen stone countertops to protect from heat and scratches.
- **Regularly seal natural stone** to maintain its luster and protect against moisture.
- **Promptly clean spills**—especially from acidic substances—to prevent staining.
