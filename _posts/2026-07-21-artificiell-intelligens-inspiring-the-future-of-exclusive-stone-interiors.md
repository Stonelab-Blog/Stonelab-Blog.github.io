## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

When curating exclusive stone interiors, consider the following:

### Kitchen Stone Countertops
- **Granite** offers exceptional durability for high-traffic kitchens and resists stains and scratches.
- **Marble** adds a refined, luminous touch—ideal for showpiece islands or breakfast bars, best suited for lower-traffic zones due to its softer nature.
- **Quartzite** blends the beauty of marble with the resilience of granite, a popular choice for both style and practicality.

### Marble Bathroom Design
- Select softer hues and gentle veining for a spa-like atmosphere.
- Large-format stone slabs reduce grout lines, enhancing visual flow and ease of cleaning.

### Bespoke Stone Furniture
- Opt for unique shapes and finishes to create custom pieces that anchor living spaces, entryways, or luxury offices.

No matter the application, StoneLab’s design experts guide clients in selecting stones that match their lifestyle, aesthetic preferences, and the specific requirements of each room.

## Caring for Your Luxury Interior Materials

Maintaining the beauty of stone surfaces is straightforward with a few essential practices:

- **Daily Cleaning**: Use a pH-neutral cleaner and a soft cloth to wipe surfaces.
- **Protection**: Always use coasters and trivets to prevent staining from acidic substances.
- **Sealing**: Periodically reseal natural stone as recommended to protect against moisture and wear.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals**: Steer clear of abrasive or acidic cleaners, which can damage the surface.

With proper care, StoneLab’s precision-crafted stonework retains its allure for generations.
