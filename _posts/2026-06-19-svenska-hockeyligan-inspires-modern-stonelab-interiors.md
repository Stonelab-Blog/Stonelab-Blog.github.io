## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the ideal stone is crucial to achieving both aesthetic harmony and lasting performance. Here are a few expert tips from StoneLab:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable, non-porous stones such as granite or engineered quartz for countertops. These materials withstand heavy use and maintain their luster.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble remains a classic choice for its luxurious appearance, especially in vanity tops and feature walls. Ensure proper sealing to protect against moisture.
- **Living Areas**: Limestone and travertine offer warmth and character for fireplaces or flooring, while bespoke stone furniture adds a sculptural focal point.
- **Outdoor Spaces**: Choose stones with natural resilience to weather, such as granite or porphyry, for terraces and garden features.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

Stone surfaces are prized for their enduring appeal, but a few simple habits will ensure they remain pristine:

- **Wipe up spills promptly**, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus juice.
- **Use pH-neutral cleaners** designed for stone to avoid damage.
- **Seal natural stone surfaces** periodically as recommended by your stone specialist.
- **Avoid abrasive pads or harsh chemicals** to preserve the finish.
- **Protect high-traffic areas** with rugs or mats to minimize wear.

StoneLab provides tailored care instructions for each installation, ensuring that every custom stone solution remains as stunning as the day it was crafted.
