### Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Here are a few practical tips to guide your choices:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for dense, low-porosity stones like granite or quartzite for countertops. These materials withstand daily use and resist staining, making them perfect for culinary spaces.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble’s timeless beauty enhances luxury bathrooms, but select varieties with appropriate sealing to prevent water absorption.
- **Living areas:** For statement walls or bespoke stone furniture, consider limestone or travertine for their subtle color variations and soft textures.
- **Entryways:** Durable stones like slate or basalt can handle high foot traffic while retaining their visual appeal.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the pristine allure of luxury interior materials requires thoughtful care:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use a pH-neutral stone cleaner and avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive sponges.
- **Sealing:** Periodically seal surfaces, especially in wet or high-use areas, to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Immediate attention:** Wipe spills promptly, particularly acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching or discoloration.
