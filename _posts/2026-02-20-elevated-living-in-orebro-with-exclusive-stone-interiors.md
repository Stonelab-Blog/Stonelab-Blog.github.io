### Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the ideal stone is essential for both style and practicality. Consider these expert tips from StoneLab:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like quartzite or granite for countertops to resist stains and scratches. Choose tones that complement cabinetry and flooring for a cohesive look.  
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a favorite for its luxurious appeal, especially in shower surrounds and vanities. Ensure proper sealing to maintain its pristine surface.  
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture and fireplace surrounds made from limestone or travertine create focal points while offering lasting durability.  
- **Sustainability:** Prioritize stones with traceable origins and minimal environmental impact. StoneLab’s commitment to sustainable stone sourcing ensures your interiors are as responsible as they are beautiful.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the elegance of your stonework, follow these essential care tips:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damaging natural finishes.  
- **Sealants:** Regular sealing protects against stains and moisture, especially in kitchens and bathrooms.  
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic or abrasive products that can etch or dull the stone.  
- **Prompt Spill Cleanup:** Quickly address spills, particularly from wine, coffee, or oils on luxury interior materials.
