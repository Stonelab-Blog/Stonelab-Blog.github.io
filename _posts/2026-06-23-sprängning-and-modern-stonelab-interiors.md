## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone for your interior requires expert guidance. Here are a few practical tips for choosing the ideal material for different areas of your home:

- **Kitchen stone countertops:** Opt for hardwearing stones like granite or quartzite, which resist scratches and stains while offering timeless style.
- **Marble bathroom design:** Embrace the elegance of marble, but choose sealed and honed finishes for increased slip resistance and easier maintenance.
- **Bespoke stone furniture:** Limestone and travertine add warmth and texture to custom tables, shelving, and accent pieces, providing a bespoke touch to any room.

StoneLab’s design team works closely with clients, interior designers, and architects to ensure each project reflects individual taste and functional needs.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Luxury stone deserves the best care. To maintain the pristine appearance of your interior stone design:

- Clean surfaces with pH-neutral, stone-safe cleaners.
- Wipe spills promptly to prevent staining, especially on more porous stones like marble.
- Re-seal high-traffic surfaces periodically to preserve luster and protect against moisture.
- Use soft cloths and avoid abrasive pads to prevent surface scratches.

StoneLab provides ongoing care recommendations so your exclusive stone interiors remain flawless for years to come.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What is sprängning in stone craftsmanship?

Sprängning is the precise process of extracting and preparing natural stone slabs with care to ensure structural integrity and unique aesthetic qualities, forming the foundation for custom stone solutions.

### How does StoneLab ensure sustainable stone sourcing?

StoneLab sources stone materials exclusively from responsibly managed quarries, prioritizing environmental stewardship to provide luxury interior materials that are both beautiful and eco-conscious.

### Which stones are best for kitchen stone countertops?

Hardwearing stones such as granite and quartzite are ideal for kitchen countertops due to their resistance to scratches, stains, and their timeless style.

### How can I maintain marble surfaces in my bathroom?

To maintain marble bathroom designs, use sealed and honed finishes for slip resistance, clean with pH-neutral stone-safe products, wipe spills promptly, and reseal surfaces periodically.

### Can StoneLab create bespoke stone furniture?

Yes, StoneLab specializes in bespoke stone furniture, using materials like limestone and travertine to add warmth, texture, and a custom touch to interior spaces.
