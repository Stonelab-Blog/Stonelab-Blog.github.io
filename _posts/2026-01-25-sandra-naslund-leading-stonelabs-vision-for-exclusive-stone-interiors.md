## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Each Space

Choosing the ideal stone requires careful consideration of both style and function. Sandra Naslund recommends:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops—these materials withstand daily use and offer a sleek, modern look.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble bathroom designs remain a classic, evoking elegance and tranquility. Ensure proper sealing to maintain their pristine appearance.
- **Living Areas**: Bespoke stone furniture and accent pieces serve as conversation starters, while natural stone walls add warmth and sophistication to larger spaces.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors:

- **Regular Cleaning**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid etching or scratching.
- **Sealing**: Periodically reseal surfaces, especially in high-traffic or moisture-prone areas, to protect against stains and water damage.
- **Immediate Attention**: Address spills promptly, particularly on porous stones like marble, to prevent permanent marks.
