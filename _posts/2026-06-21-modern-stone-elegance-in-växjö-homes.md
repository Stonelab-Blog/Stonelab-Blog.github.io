## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Scandinavian Home

Selecting the perfect stone for your home is both an art and a science. Here’s how to approach the decision:

- **Kitchen Countertops:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz. These luxury interior materials are resistant to stains and scratches, ensuring longevity in busy kitchens.
- **Bathroom Retreats:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic choice, offering both elegance and water resistance. Consider honed finishes for a softer, matte look.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture and accent walls in limestone or travertine add texture and warmth to social spaces.
- **Outdoor Areas:** For patios or terraces, weather-resistant stones such as slate maintain their beauty through the seasons.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty of Your Stone Interiors

To preserve the allure of your exclusive stone interiors:

- Wipe surfaces daily with a soft, damp cloth.
- Use pH-neutral cleaners to avoid damaging the stone.
- Reseal kitchen stone countertops and high-use surfaces annually.
- Avoid harsh chemicals, especially on marble and limestone.

StoneLab advises clients on tailored maintenance routines, ensuring each surface retains its premium finish for years to come.
