- **Bathroom**: Embrace the spa-like luxury of marble bathroom design, selecting stones with natural water resistance.
- **Living Spaces**: Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables and accent walls, creates a striking focal point.
- **Entryways and Halls**: Hard-wearing limestone or terrazzo makes a bold first impression while standing up to heavy use.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Luxury demands longevity. To keep your custom stone solutions looking their best:

- Wipe surfaces regularly with a soft, damp cloth.
- Use pH-neutral cleaners designed for stone.
- Avoid abrasive pads and harsh chemicals.
- Seal stone surfaces as recommended to protect against stains and moisture.

StoneLab offers ongoing advice and maintenance services to ensure your investment remains pristine for years to come.
