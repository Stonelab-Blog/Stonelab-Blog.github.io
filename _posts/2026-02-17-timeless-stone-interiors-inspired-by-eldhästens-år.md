## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Scandinavian Home

Choosing the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are a few considerations to guide you:

- **For Kitchens**: Opt for hardwearing, low-porosity stones like granite or engineered quartz for countertops, ensuring both beauty and practicality.
- **For Bathrooms**: Marble and limestone offer a luxurious feel, but require proper sealing to maintain their pristine appearance.
- **For Living Areas**: Statement pieces in travertine or bespoke stone furniture can add texture and warmth.

StoneLab’s experts provide tailored guidance, helping you select the ideal material and finish to harmonize with your vision and the unique characteristics of your space.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Your Exclusive Stone Interiors

To preserve the natural splendor of your interior stone design:

- Clean regularly with pH-neutral cleaners
- Avoid harsh chemicals that may erode the surface
- Reseal marble and limestone periodically to protect against stains
- Use coasters and mats on kitchen stone countertops to prevent water marks and scratches

With thoughtful care, your StoneLab surfaces will retain their radiance for generations.
