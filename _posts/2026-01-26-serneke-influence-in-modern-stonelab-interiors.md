## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Luxury Homes and Commercial Projects

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are a few guidelines for choosing materials that harmonize with your vision:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust materials like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops. These surfaces resist stains and scratches, combining beauty with practicality.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a classic for marble bathroom design, providing a sense of spa-like luxury. Select sealed or honed finishes for added slip resistance and easier care.
- **Living Spaces:** For bespoke stone furniture or accent walls, consider limestone or travertine for their warm tones and subtle patterns.

StoneLab’s expert team offers tailored consultations, ensuring that your chosen stone aligns perfectly with your design and lifestyle.

## Maintaining the Beauty of Stone Interiors in the UK and Scandinavia

To preserve the elegance of your interior stone design, follow these care tips:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to prevent etching or dullness.
- **Sealing:** Depending on the stone type, periodic sealing protects against moisture and stains.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe spills promptly, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus.

Our specialists at StoneLab provide comprehensive guidance to help clients maintain the enduring beauty of their exclusive stone interiors.
