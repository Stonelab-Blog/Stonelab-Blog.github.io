### Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Consider these factors when crafting your exclusive stone interiors:

#### For Kitchens
- **Granite and quartzite:** Ideal for kitchen stone countertops, these stones offer superior durability and resistance to stains.
- **Marble:** Best suited for statement islands or backsplashes, bringing unmatched elegance with proper sealing and care.

#### For Bathrooms
- **Marble and limestone:** Favored for their refined appearance, perfect for marble bathroom design and spa-inspired spaces.
- **Porcelain stoneware:** A practical choice for high-moisture areas, combining aesthetic appeal with easy maintenance.

#### For Living Spaces and Furniture
- **Custom stone furniture:** Tables, benches, and shelving crafted from unique slabs become sculptural focal points.
- **Accent walls and fireplaces:** Natural stone cladding adds depth and warmth to lounges and bedrooms.

### Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty

To preserve the allure of your stone surfaces:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to maintain the stone’s finish.
- **Protective sealing:** Schedule professional sealing to enhance stain resistance, especially for high-traffic areas.
- **Prompt spill management:** Quickly wipe away acidic or oily spills to prevent staining.
- **Gentle handling:** Avoid abrasive tools and harsh chemicals that can dull or scratch the stone.

StoneLab provides ongoing support and tailored guidance to help clients keep their luxury surfaces immaculate year after year.
