## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space in Northern Europe

Selecting the perfect stone involves balancing aesthetics, function, and longevity. Here are a few expert tips:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust materials like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops—resistant to stains and scratches while exuding elegance.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble and limestone create a tranquil atmosphere but benefit from sealed finishes to prevent watermarks.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture in travertine or terrazzo adds texture and sophistication.
- **Accent Features:** Dramatic onyx or bold-veined marble make for unforgettable feature walls or fireplace surrounds.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the luxury of your interior stone design:

- **Clean gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to prevent etching or dullness.
- **Seal regularly:** Reapply sealants as recommended to enhance resistance to spills and stains.
- **Protect from heat and impact:** Always use trivets, coasters, and cutting boards.
- **Address spills promptly:** Especially with marble and limestone, blot spills quickly to avoid staining.
