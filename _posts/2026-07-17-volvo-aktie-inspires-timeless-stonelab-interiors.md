## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the ideal stone for each room is a blend of artistry and practicality. Here are StoneLab’s recommendations for various living spaces:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for robust, easy-to-maintain surfaces like granite or engineered stone for kitchen stone countertops. Their resilience stands up to daily use while exuding sophistication.  
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains the pinnacle of luxury. Choose water-resistant stones and consider honed finishes to prevent slipping.  
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or feature walls, can serve as elegant focal points. Travertine and quartzite are excellent for their unique textures.  
- **Entryways:** Durable stones with striking patterns make grand impressions in entrance halls, setting the tone for the entire home.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Stone Surfaces

Luxury stone requires thoughtful care to preserve its natural beauty:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use a mild, pH-neutral cleaner and soft cloth to avoid damaging the surface.  
- **Sealing:** Many natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to protect against stains and moisture.  
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe spills promptly, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching.  
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals and abrasive sponges that can dull the finish.
