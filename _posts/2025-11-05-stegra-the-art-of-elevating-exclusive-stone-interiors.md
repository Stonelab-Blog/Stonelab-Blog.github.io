## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Sweden and Scandinavia

Choosing the ideal stone requires an understanding of both function and style. Here are a few considerations to ensure lasting satisfaction:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable, non-porous stones like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops. These materials withstand heavy use while maintaining their luxurious appearance.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble, with its timeless allure, is perfect for creating tranquil retreats. Ensure proper sealing to protect against moisture.
- **Living Areas**: Limestone and travertine offer warmth and subtle elegance for flooring and wall features.
- **Furniture**: Bespoke stone furniture can be tailored to complement your space, whether it’s a dramatic dining table or a minimalist bench.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces: Tips from StoneLab Experts

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors:

- Wipe up spills promptly to prevent staining, especially on marble and limestone.
- Use pH-neutral cleaners designed for natural stone.
- Reseal surfaces periodically to maintain resistance to moisture and stains.
- Avoid abrasive tools that can scratch or dull the finish.

By following these simple maintenance steps, your stone surfaces will retain their radiant appeal for decades.
