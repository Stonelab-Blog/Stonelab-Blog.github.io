## Current Trends in Interior Stone Design in Sweden and Scandinavia

- **Sculptural Forms:** Designers are embracing organic shapes and flowing lines in stone, creating custom pieces that blur the boundaries between art and function.

## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Northern Europe and the UK

Selecting the ideal stone involves balancing durability, aesthetics, and maintenance. Here are StoneLab’s expert tips for making the right choice:

- **Kitchens:** Engineered quartz and dense granites are ideal for kitchen stone countertops, offering both resilience and striking beauty.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic, but consider sealed limestones or quartzites for high-traffic areas to ensure long-lasting elegance.
- **Living Spaces:** For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, opt for stones with unique veining or rare colorations—StoneLab’s custom stone solutions make every piece one of a kind.
- **Outdoor Areas:** Choose frost-resistant stones and consider anti-slip finishes for terraces and pool surrounds, ensuring safety and style in all seasons.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty of exclusive stone interiors, follow these essential care guidelines:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to prevent dulling or scratching the surface.
- **Seal Periodically:** Natural stones benefit from regular sealing, especially in kitchens and bathrooms, to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic or abrasive products, which can damage even the most durable stones.
- **Address Spills Promptly:** Wipe up spills immediately, particularly on marble and limestone, to prevent etching or staining.
