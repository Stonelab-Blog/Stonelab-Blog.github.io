## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty of Stone Interiors

Stone surfaces are remarkably resilient, but proper care preserves their allure:

- **Regular Cleaning**: Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to avoid surface damage.
- **Protection**: Always use coasters, especially on kitchen stone countertops, to prevent staining.
- **Sealing**: Periodic sealing fortifies natural stone against moisture and spills—StoneLab provides guidance tailored to each material.
- **Immediate Action**: Wipe spills promptly to maintain the surface’s integrity.
