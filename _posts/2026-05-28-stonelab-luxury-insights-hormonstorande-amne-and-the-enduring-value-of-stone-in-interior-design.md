## Selecting the Right Stone for Each Space

Choosing the perfect stone requires balancing beauty, function, and sustainability:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable granites or quartzites for kitchen stone countertops—these resist staining and scratching, and their non-porous nature helps minimize exposure to hormonstorande amne.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble bathroom design remains a classic, but consider sealed stones to protect against moisture while preserving natural elegance.
- **Living Spaces**: Bespoke stone furniture, such as a statement coffee table or sculptural shelving, introduces a sense of artistry and permanence.
- **Entryways and Hallways**: Hardwearing stones like limestone or slate withstand heavy traffic while contributing to a welcoming first impression.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty

StoneLab’s precision-crafted stonework is designed for longevity, but simple care routines will keep your surfaces immaculate:

- **Daily Cleaning**: Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to avoid residue and protect the stone’s finish.
- **Sealing**: Periodic sealing enhances stain resistance, especially for marble and lighter-toned stones.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals**: Steer clear of acidic or abrasive cleaners that may damage the stone or compromise its natural properties.
