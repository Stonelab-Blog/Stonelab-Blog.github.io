## Practical Advice for Selecting the Right Stone

Selecting the ideal stone for your home or project involves more than choosing a color. Here are key considerations:

- **Functionality:** Kitchens benefit from robust materials like granite or engineered stone, while bathrooms shine with marble’s luxurious sheen.
- **Aesthetic Harmony:** Match stone textures and tones to your overall design palette for a cohesive feel.
- **Sustainability:** Choose providers like StoneLab who prioritize sustainable stone sourcing, ensuring your investment is both beautiful and responsible.

## Care and Maintenance Tips for Lasting Beauty

To keep your stone surfaces pristine:

- **Regular Cleaning:** Use a gentle, pH-neutral cleaner to avoid damaging the stone’s surface.
- **Sealant Application:** Periodically seal porous stones like marble to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Immediate Attention:** Address spills quickly, especially acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching.
