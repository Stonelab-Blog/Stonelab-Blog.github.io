### Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space in Scandinavian Luxury Homes

Selecting the perfect stone for your interior requires a blend of aesthetic vision and practical consideration. Here are StoneLab’s expert tips tailored for luxury homeowners and designers in Scandinavia and beyond:

- **Kitchens**: Opt for durable stones such as granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops. These materials offer both resilience and timeless elegance.
- **Bathrooms**: Marble is a classic choice for marble bathroom design. Its natural beauty and versatility create an indulgent spa-like atmosphere.
- **Living Areas**: Limestone and travertine add warmth and subtle texture to floors and wall cladding, enhancing comfort and sophistication.
- **Furniture**: Bespoke stone furniture crafted from exotic materials like onyx or rare marbles becomes a striking centerpiece in any space.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces to Preserve Luxury Interiors

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors, follow these maintenance guidelines:

- **Seal Regularly**: High-quality sealants protect against stains and moisture, especially in high-use areas such as kitchens and bathrooms.
- **Gentle Cleaning**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damaging the stone’s surface.
- **Avoid Abrasives**: Never use harsh chemicals or abrasive pads, as they can scratch or dull the finish.
- **Address Spills Promptly**: Particularly for materials like marble, immediate cleaning prevents staining and etching.
