## Selecting the Right Stone for Your Space

Choosing the ideal stone is both an art and a science. Here are StoneLab’s expert recommendations:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for resilient stones like granite or quartzite for countertops, combining beauty with practicality.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble offers classic elegance, especially in Scandinavian-inspired spa environments—ensure it is sealed to protect against moisture.
- **Living Areas:** Limestone and travertine add subtle warmth to floors and walls, while bespoke stone furniture can anchor open-plan spaces.
- **Outdoor Spaces:** Consider frost-resistant materials for terraces and patios to withstand Northern European climates.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the signature allure of your interior stone design, follow these essential care tips:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to prevent etching or scratching.
- **Sealing:** Periodically seal porous stones like marble and limestone to guard against stains and moisture.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Steer clear of harsh chemicals and abrasive pads, especially on polished and textured finishes.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Quickly wipe up spills, particularly acidic substances, to maintain pristine surfaces.
