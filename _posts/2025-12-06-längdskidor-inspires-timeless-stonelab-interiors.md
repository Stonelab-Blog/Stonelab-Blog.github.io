## Choosing the Right Stone for Your Space Across Scandinavia and Beyond

Selecting the perfect stone is essential to achieving both function and beauty. Consider these expert tips:

- **For Kitchens:** Opt for robust materials like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops, combining resilience with elegance.
- **In Bathrooms:** Choose marble or limestone for a spa-like atmosphere, ensuring surfaces are sealed for moisture protection.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture and feature walls can serve as a focal point—travertine or onyx add warmth and character.

StoneLab’s specialists guide clients in matching stone type, finish, and color to the specific requirements of each space, ensuring harmony and impact.

## Care and Maintenance for Lasting Beauty of Your Custom Stone Solutions

To preserve the exquisite finish of your interior stone design:

- **Clean Regularly:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid etching or scratching surfaces.
- **Seal When Needed:** Periodic sealing enhances stain resistance, especially in high-use areas.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic or abrasive products that can damage natural stone.

With proper care, StoneLab’s custom stone solutions deliver enduring beauty and performance.
