### Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are some expert tips to guide your decisions:

- **Consider usage and lifestyle**: For high-traffic areas like kitchens, opt for hard-wearing stones such as Kvinnersta limestone or granite. In bathrooms, marble and limestone offer a touch of luxury while remaining practical.
- **Tailor to your aesthetic**: Light, neutral tones create a spacious feel, while darker stones add drama and sophistication.
- **Bespoke solutions**: Work with a partner like StoneLab to design custom stone solutions that fit your exact specifications, from exclusive stone interiors to one-of-a-kind furniture.

### Caring for Stone Surfaces

Premium stone surfaces are an investment in beauty and longevity. To keep them at their best:

- **Seal regularly**: Especially in moisture-prone areas, to prevent staining.
- **Clean gently**: Use pH-neutral cleaners and avoid harsh chemicals.
- **Protect from impact**: Always use cutting boards and trivets in kitchens.

StoneLab provides comprehensive care guidance, ensuring your surfaces retain their elegance for generations.
