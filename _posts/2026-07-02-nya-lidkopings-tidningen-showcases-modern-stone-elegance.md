### Current Trends in Interior Stone Design

The resurgence of stone in interior design is driven by a desire for natural luxury and individuality. Some of the most sought-after trends in exclusive stone interiors include:

- **Monolithic Kitchen Islands:** Large-format kitchen stone countertops in marble or granite make a dramatic centerpiece, combining function with sculptural appeal.
- **Textured Finishes:** Honed and leathered surfaces add tactile interest and soften the stone’s appearance, making it inviting to the touch.
- **Seamless Transitions:** Integrated stone sinks, backsplashes, and wall panels create a continuous flow, emphasizing craftsmanship and attention to detail.
- **Bespoke Stone Furniture:** Custom tables, shelving, and accent pieces crafted from stone provide distinctive focal points in living and dining spaces.

### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Room

Choosing the ideal stone for your project is both an art and a science. Here are some expert tips from the StoneLab team:

- **For Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite, which can withstand heavy use. Lighter tones brighten the space, while dramatic veining creates visual interest.
- **For Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a symbol of opulence. Select stones with low porosity and proper sealing to ensure longevity in moist environments.
- **For Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture or feature walls in travertine or limestone add warmth and textural contrast to contemporary interiors.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To maintain the beauty of your custom stone solutions:

- **Clean regularly** with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals** which can etch natural stone surfaces.
- **Seal periodically** based on the type of stone and level of use, especially in kitchens and bathrooms.
- **Address spills promptly** to prevent staining, particularly on marble and lighter stones.
