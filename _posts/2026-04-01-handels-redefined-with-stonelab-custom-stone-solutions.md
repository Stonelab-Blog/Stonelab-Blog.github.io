## The Enduring Value of Stone in Interior Design

Stone is synonymous with permanence and prestige. Its tactile qualities and visual depth enhance homes, transforming everyday handels—be it a door pull, drawer front, or cabinetry interface—into exquisite focal points. Interior stone design now goes beyond countertops and flooring. Today’s trend highlights bespoke stone furniture and refined handels, crafted to perfection and tailored for each space.

### Trending: Stone as Functional Accent

Modern luxury interiors embrace statement handels made from marble, granite, and other premium stones. These features combine practicality with artistry, offering:

- **Unmatched durability:** Stone handels withstand daily use, retaining their beauty for generations.
- **Timeless sophistication:** Natural variations in texture and tone elevate even the smallest design details.
- **Seamless integration:** Custom stone solutions allow for perfect harmony with kitchen stone countertops, marble bathroom designs, and other interior elements.

## Choosing the Perfect Stone for Your Handels

Selecting the right stone is essential for both aesthetics and performance. Consider these expert tips from StoneLab:

- **Match stone type to function:** For high-use handels, opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite. For decorative handels in less trafficked areas, marble offers unparalleled elegance.
- **Coordinate with existing materials:** Harmonize handels with luxury interior materials, such as bespoke stone furniture or stone feature walls, for a cohesive look.
- **Opt for exclusive finishes:** Polished, honed, or textured surfaces can dramatically alter the feel and style of handels, reflecting your unique taste.

## Maintaining the Beauty of Stone Features

StoneLab’s precision-crafted stonework is designed to last, but thoughtful care ensures surfaces remain pristine:

- **Regular cleaning:** Wipe handels and stone surfaces with a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage finishes.
- **Protect from heavy impact:** Use gentle handling, especially for marble bathroom design accents and bespoke stone furniture pieces.
- **Periodic sealing:** Depending on the stone type, periodic sealing helps resist stains and moisture, preserving the exclusive stone interiors’ allure.
