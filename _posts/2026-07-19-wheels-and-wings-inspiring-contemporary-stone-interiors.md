## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space in Luxury Homes and Developments

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Consider these insights for your next project:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust stones like granite or quartzite for countertops, combining resilience with elegance. For a bold statement, explore marble varieties with distinctive veining.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble and travertine elevate bathroom design, offering a spa-like atmosphere and timeless appeal.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture or feature fireplaces in limestone or sandstone add character and warmth.
- **Entryways:** Durable stones such as slate or basalt make a lasting first impression while withstanding high foot traffic.

## Maintaining the Beauty of Your Stone Interiors with StoneLab’s Expertise

To keep your stone surfaces pristine:

- **Clean Regularly:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and soft cloths to avoid damage.
- **Seal Periodically:** High-contact surfaces benefit from sealing to prevent stains and moisture absorption.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Gentle care preserves the natural finish and intricate detailing of Swedish stone craftsmanship.
