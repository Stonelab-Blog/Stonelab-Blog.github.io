### Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space in Your Scandinavian or Northern European Home

Choosing the perfect stone requires balancing aesthetics, functionality, and maintenance needs. Here are StoneLab’s recommendations for luxury interior materials across key living spaces:

#### Kitchen

- **Best choice:** Durable natural stones such as granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops.
- **Benefits:** Resistant to heat and scratches; available in a spectrum of finishes from polished to honed.
- **Tip:** Opt for lighter shades to reflect natural light and enlarge the sense of space, ideal for Nordic homes.

#### Bathroom

- **Best choice:** Marble for a classic, spa-like marble bathroom design.
- **Benefits:** Luxurious appearance; cool underfoot; available in exquisite veining and color options.
- **Tip:** Consider honed finishes and sealed surfaces to enhance slip resistance and protect against moisture, important in humid bathroom environments.

#### Living Spaces

- **Best choice:** Limestone, travertine, or exclusive granites for bespoke stone furniture and accent walls.
- **Benefits:** Adds warmth, texture, and a unique design statement.
- **Tip:** Match stone tones to your palette and use large-format pieces for a seamless, contemporary effect.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces in Scandinavian Climates

To keep your exclusive stone interiors immaculate, routine care is essential:

- **Regular cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner—avoid acidic agents that may etch or dull the surface.
- **Sealing:** Periodically re-seal porous stones to prevent staining and maintain water resistance.
- **Quick response:** Blot spills immediately, especially on marble, to avoid marks from acidic foods and beverages.
- **Professional maintenance:** Schedule periodic assessments with stone specialists for deep cleaning and refinishing.

### Which types of stone are best for kitchen countertops in luxury homes?

Granite and quartzite are ideal for kitchen stone countertops due to their durability, heat resistance, and broad range of finishes. StoneLab recommends lighter shades to enhance natural light in kitchens typical of Northern Europe.
