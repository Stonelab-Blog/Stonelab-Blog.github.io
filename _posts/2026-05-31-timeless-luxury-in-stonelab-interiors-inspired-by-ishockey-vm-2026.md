## Selecting the Right Stone for Every Space

Choosing the perfect stone is an art. Here are expert tips from StoneLab:

- **Kitchen:** Opt for dense, non-porous stones like granite or quartzite for countertops that resist stains and scratches.
- **Bathroom:** Marble’s luminous finish is ideal for vanities and walls, but ensure proper sealing for longevity.
- **Living areas:** Bespoke stone furniture in travertine or limestone adds sophistication and a tactile element.
- **Feature walls:** Dramatic veining or textured finishes can turn an ordinary wall into a design focal point.

## Care and Maintenance for Enduring Beauty

Custom stone solutions are an investment—proper care ensures their allure endures:

- **Daily cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to protect surfaces.
- **Sealing:** Periodic sealing prevents staining and preserves the stone’s natural luster.
- **Avoid harsh chemicals:** Abrasives and acids can damage even the most durable surfaces.
- **Prompt attention:** Wipe spills immediately, especially on marble and limestone.
