### Selecting the Perfect Stone for Every Space

Choosing the right stone is pivotal to both aesthetics and functionality. Here are some practical tips:

- **Kitchen Countertops:** Opt for dense, non-porous stones like granite or engineered quartz for durability and ease of maintenance. Consider the finish—honed surfaces hide fingerprints, while polished ones amplify light.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a favourite for its soft elegance. Ensure proper sealing and choose stones with low porosity for wet areas.
- **Living Spaces:** Bespoke stone furniture or fireplace surrounds create a luxurious statement. Select stone colours that complement your broader palette for harmony.
- **Entryways and Stairs:** Hard-wearing stones such as basalt or granite offer both beauty and resilience in high-traffic areas.

### Caring for Your StoneLab Surfaces

Maintaining the timeless appeal of your exclusive stone interiors is simple with the right approach:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to keep surfaces spotless.
- **Sealing:** Regularly seal natural stone to prevent staining and moisture absorption, especially in kitchens and bathrooms.
- **Avoid Abrasives:** Refrain from using harsh chemicals or abrasive pads, which can dull the surface.
- **Prompt Spill Management:** Wipe spills immediately, particularly acidic substances like wine or citrus, to preserve the stone’s finish.
