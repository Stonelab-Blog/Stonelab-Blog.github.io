## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Space

Selecting the perfect stone is both an art and a science. Here are practical considerations for different areas of your home:

### Kitchen

- **Countertops:** Granite and quartzite offer unmatched durability and stain resistance, making them ideal for kitchen stone countertops.
- **Backsplashes:** Marble adds a touch of luxury and pairs beautifully with contemporary cabinetry.

### Bathroom

- **Walls and Floors:** Consider marble or travertine for a spa-like atmosphere that exudes exclusivity.
- **Vanities:** Choose stones with subtle veining for an elegant, cohesive look.

### Living Areas

- **Fireplace Surrounds:** Statement stone creates a striking focal point.
- **Custom Furniture:** Bespoke stone furniture, such as coffee tables or console tops, makes a luxurious impression.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To keep your exclusive stone interiors pristine, follow these expert tips:

- **Daily Wipe-Down:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner to remove dust and spills.
- **Seal Regularly:** Protect porous stones like marble and limestone with high-quality sealant.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic or abrasive cleaning agents.
- **Prompt Cleanup:** Address spills quickly, especially with wine, citrus, or oils, to prevent staining.
