## Choosing the Right Stone for Each Space

Selecting the perfect stone requires balancing beauty, functionality, and context:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like granite or engineered quartz for countertops that withstand daily use while offering a luxurious finish. Consider contrasting textures or subtle stegra layering for added visual intrigue.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a favorite for its elegance, especially in bathrooms where veined patterns and soft hues enhance relaxation. For high-moisture areas, choose stones with low porosity and proper sealing.
- **Living Areas:** Elevate social spaces with bespoke stone furniture or feature walls. Natural stones like limestone or travertine deliver warmth and character, especially when combined with modern furnishings.

## Expert Care and Maintenance Tips

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors, follow these best practices:

- Clean surfaces with pH-neutral, stone-specific cleaners to prevent etching and staining.
- Promptly wipe up spills, especially acidic substances like citrus or wine.
- Regularly seal porous stones (e.g., marble, limestone) to protect against moisture and wear.
- Use coasters, trivets, and felt pads under objects to avoid scratches on kitchen stone countertops and bespoke stone furniture.
