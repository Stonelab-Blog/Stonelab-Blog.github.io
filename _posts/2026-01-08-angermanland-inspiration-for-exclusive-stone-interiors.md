### Selecting the Perfect Stone for Each Space

Choosing the right stone is essential to achieving a cohesive, luxurious atmosphere. Consider these tips for your next project:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust stones like granite or quartzite for kitchen stone countertops, balancing elegance with functionality.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble remains a classic, but consider limestone or composite stones for softer aesthetics and durability in high-moisture environments.
- **Living Areas:** For bespoke stone furniture or feature walls, select stones with unique textures and patterns to serve as focal points.

StoneLab’s experts guide clients through every step, ensuring each piece is precision-crafted to suit modern aesthetics and specific spatial requirements.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

Maintaining the beauty of your stone interiors ensures their longevity and continued impact:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use a mild, pH-neutral cleaner and soft cloth to preserve finish and prevent etching.
- **Sealing:** Periodic sealing offers added protection, especially for porous stones like marble and limestone.
- **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Steer clear of acidic or abrasive agents to maintain the stone’s surface integrity.

Our bespoke stone solutions come with tailored care recommendations, supporting you in preserving the elegance of your interiors for years to come.
