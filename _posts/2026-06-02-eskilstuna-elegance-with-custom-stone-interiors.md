### Selecting the Perfect Stone for Every Space

Choosing the right stone is both an art and a science. Here are key considerations for various areas:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for durable stones like granite or quartzite for countertops—resistant to heat, stains, and daily use, while providing luxurious appeal.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a favorite for its natural luminosity and spa-like ambiance. For a modern twist, consider large-format tiles or seamless stone vanities.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture, from statement coffee tables to sculptural shelving, introduces elegant functionality and unique character.
- **Entryways and Hallways:** Use exclusive stone interiors to create a lasting first impression with grand flooring or architectural accents.

### Caring for Your Stone Surfaces

To preserve the beauty and longevity of your custom stone solutions, follow these expert tips:

- **Routine Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth and pH-neutral cleaner; avoid harsh chemicals that can damage the stone’s finish.
- **Sealing:** Many natural stones benefit from periodic sealing to prevent staining and maintain their surface integrity.
- **Immediate Attention:** Wipe up spills promptly, especially on marble and limestone, to avoid etching or discoloration.
- **Professional Maintenance:** For bespoke pieces and high-traffic areas, schedule regular professional care to keep stone interiors looking pristine year after year.
