## Current Stone Trends for Luxury Spaces in Sweden and Northern Europe

This season, the design narrative is all about bold contrasts and organic forms. Here are a few trends shaping high-end interiors across Sweden, Scandinavia, Germany, and the UK:

- **Monolithic Surfaces:** Seamless, large-format slabs for kitchens and bathrooms evoke a sense of grandeur and continuity.
- **Textured Finishes:** Honed, leathered, or fluted stone surfaces add tactile richness to walls, floors, and bespoke stone furniture.
- **Sustainable Stone Sourcing:** Eco-conscious homeowners and designers are prioritizing ethically sourced materials with a low environmental footprint.
- **Unique Colorways:** Deep greens, dramatic veining, and subtle earth tones are redefining what luxury interior materials can be.

## Selecting the Perfect Stone for Each Space: Custom Stone Solutions Tailored to Your Needs

Every room has its own requirements when it comes to stonework. Here’s how to choose wisely:

- **Kitchens:** Opt for robust stones like granite or engineered quartz for kitchen stone countertops that resist stains and scratches.
- **Bathrooms:** Marble bathroom design remains a classic choice, but consider non-porous varieties or protective sealants for added longevity.
- **Living Areas:** Bespoke stone furniture—think sculptural coffee tables or mantelpieces—creates an unforgettable focal point.
- **Entryways:** Durable, slip-resistant stones such as limestone or slate are ideal for high-traffic areas.

## Stone Care and Maintenance Tips for Exclusive Stone Interiors

To preserve the beauty of your exclusive stone interiors crafted by StoneLab:

- **Clean Gently:** Use pH-neutral cleaners and avoid harsh abrasives.
- **Seal Regularly:** Protect porous stones with periodic sealing to guard against stains.
- **Wipe Spills Promptly:** Especially with acidic substances like wine or citrus, to prevent etching.
- **Use Mats and Trivets:** Shield surfaces from heavy cookware and high temperatures.
