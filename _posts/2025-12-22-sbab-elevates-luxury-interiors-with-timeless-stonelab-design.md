## Choosing the Right Stone for Every Luxury Interior Space

Selecting the ideal stone involves balancing style, function, and sustainability. Here are StoneLab’s expert recommendations:

### For Kitchens: Kitchen Stone Countertops Crafted with Swedish Stone Craftsmanship

- **Kitchen Stone Countertops:** Granite and quartzite offer outstanding resilience and stain resistance, perfect for culinary spaces.
- **Finish Matters:** Choose a honed or matte finish for a softer, contemporary look that reduces the appearance of fingerprints.

### For Bathrooms: Marble Bathroom Design for Exclusive Stone Interiors

- **Marble Bathroom Design:** Marble brings a spa-like ambience and unmatched luxury. Opt for sealed marble to guard against moisture and stains.
- **Accents:** Consider integrating stone sinks or shelving for subtle elegance.

### For Living Spaces: Bespoke Stone Furniture and Feature Walls

- **Bespoke Stone Furniture:** Coffee tables, benches, and shelving units designed in exclusive stone create focal points and conversation pieces.
- **Feature Walls:** Natural stone cladding or fireplace surrounds add warmth and architectural interest.

## Caring for Your Stone Surfaces: Tips from StoneLab

To preserve the beauty of your interior stone design:

- **Daily Cleaning:** Use a soft cloth with mild, pH-neutral soap; avoid acidic or abrasive cleaners.
- **Sealing:** Periodically seal natural stone surfaces to protect against stains and moisture.
- **Prompt Attention:** Wipe up spills immediately—especially oils, wine, or citrus—to prevent etching or discoloration.
